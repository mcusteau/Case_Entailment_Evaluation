This is an application under subsection 72 (1) of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act, SC 2001, c 27 [the IRPA] in which Qiuyou Zheng [Mr. Zheng] seeks judicial review of a decision dated August 13, 2018 [the Decision] by the Refugee Protection Division [the RPD]. In its decision, the RPD concluded that Mr. Zheng is neither a Convention refugee under section 96 of the IRP, nor a person in need of protection within the meaning of paragraphs 97(1)(a) or (b) of the IRPA.
