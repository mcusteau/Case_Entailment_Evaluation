As the Federal Court of Appeal noted in
Canadian Tire Corp. v. P.S. Partsource Inc.
, [2001] F.C.J. No. 181; 200 F.T.R. 94 (F.C.A.), Rule 81(1) is a rule of practice and procedure and so does not displace the common-law exceptions to the hearsay rule, including the reliability and necessity exception. However, because Rule 81 is a rule of procedure, in appropriate, and likely unusual, circumstances where a party wishes to introduce hearsay evidence that party should, at least, put forward evidence to support the arguments of reliability and necessity.
