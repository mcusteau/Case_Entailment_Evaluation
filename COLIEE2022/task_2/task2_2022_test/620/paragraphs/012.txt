The failure of an application to be supported by affidavits based on personal knowledge has been held not to result automatically in dismissal of an application for judicial review: see:
Huang v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
, [1998] F.T.R. Uned. 572; [1998] F.C.J. No. 788 (T.D.);
Moldevan v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
(1999), 235 N.R. 192 (F.C.A.);
Ominayak et al. v. Lubicon Lake Indian Nation
, [2000] F.C.J. No. 247; 185 F.T.R. 33 (T.D.), reversed without comment on this point (2000), 267 N.R. 96 (F.C.A.).
