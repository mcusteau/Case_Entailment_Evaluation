The government of Sindh thereafter started an inquiry into Ms. Hayat’s rape, which Mr. Moin says was intended as retribution against the officers who investigated the case. Mr. Moin was told he would go to prison, and authorities seized several pieces of his land. Afraid for his safety and his family’s, Mr. Moin moved to Lahore. As a result of political changes, the investigation against Mr. Moin was closed and he was eventually cleared of all charges. He retired in December 1993.
