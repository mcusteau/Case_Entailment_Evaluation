In the early 1980's Mr. Al Yamani applied for admission to Canada as a landed immigrant while he was living in Lebanon. Ultimately his application was approved. He arrived in Canada and was granted landing on April 27, 1985. He has resided here since then, making a number of trips to the Middle East and to the United States from time to time. He is married and has two children born in Canada, one from his present marriage and one from a former marriage.
