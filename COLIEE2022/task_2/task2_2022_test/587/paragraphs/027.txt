Federal Court Act

Act
the Trial Division of this court, except in relation to proceedings of bodies or agencies specified in s. 28, has exclusive jurisdiction to hear and determine an application for relief against any federal board, commission or tribunal, and that relief may only be obtained by an application for judicial review under s. 18.1. The latter section provides for applications for judicial review, in the following terms.
"18.1(1) An application for judicial review may be made by the Attorney General of Canada or by anyone directly affected by the matter in respect of which relief is sought.
