On January 15, 2004, the Applicant made a claim for refugee protection in Canada pursuant to sections 96 and 97 of the Act. He based his refugee claim on his political activities as a member of the Bangladesh Chatra League (BCL), which is a branch of the Bangladesh Awami League (AL). The AL was the governing party of Bangladesh from 1996 until its defeat in the October 2001 election.
