The plaintiffs commenced an action in this court by way of Statement of Claim dated December 2, 1991, wherein they alleged that the defendant was infringing their Canadian Patent No. 980,688. The defendant filed a Statement of Defence and Counterclaim, dated February 3, 1992, denying the allegations of infringement and seeking a declaration that the plaintiffs' patent was invalid on the grounds set out in the defendant's Particulars of Objection.
