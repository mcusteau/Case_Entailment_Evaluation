The background events leading to this decision may be summarized. The applicant arrived in Canada in September 1988 as a visitor with authorization to attend school in Canada. In December 1990 he had apparently withdrawn from his studies and in April 1991 he was under investigation by immigration officers for working in Canada without authorization. On August 1, 1991, he was issued a Minister's permit authorizing him to remain in Canada for one year, on the basis that he had earlier married a Canadian citizen who initiated application to sponsor him pending processing of an application for permanent residence made by him from within Canada.
