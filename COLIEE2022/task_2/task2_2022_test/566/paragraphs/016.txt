The Board noted that the claimant attempted to establish a link between the bandits of 1993 and the guerillas who had come looking for him nine years previously. He stated that his sister Elda who had seen the guerillas several times at their home in 1983, when she was 12 years old, had recognized the voices of the hooded men and had identified them as the former guerillas. In addition, she said, one of the hooded bandits was tall as one of the guerillas had been.
