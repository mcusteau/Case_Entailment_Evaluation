The applicant and Mr. Amir Ali attended interviews on March 3, 1997 at the Canadian High Commission in Islamabad, Pakistan. They were interviewed by Immigration Programme Officer Vonne Solis who made certain findings including that Wazir Canada only existed on paper, that there were three Canadian directors, but no physical company and no employees and that the applicant and Mr. Amir Ali did not intend to enter Canada for a temporary period. The applicant also stated that there was a possibility that his businesses in Pakistan may be sold in the future. Officer Solis concluded that they were not eligible for employment authorizations.
