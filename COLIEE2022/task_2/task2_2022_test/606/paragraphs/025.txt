As mentioned, the Board's conclusion on this issue derived, in part, from the oral testimony of Toronto police officers who described their working definition of a "gang" and their knowledge of the activities of certain Tamil groups in Toronto. Detective Constable Fernandes, an expert on Tamil gangs, said that a gang was a group of people who "work together towards a criminal and antisocial behaviour, primarily to a defined area where they create intimidation and fear in that community".
