As for the respondent's submissions, he first criticizes the IRB for failing to analyze the criterion of membership in a particular social group, i.e. the family. According to him, the IRB's analysis did not dispute that he is a member of the Bakary family which, according to the evidence, has suffered persecution: several members of his family have had to seek refuge abroad, and a number of them have been received in Canada as refugees.
