The respondent submits that there was no error of law, that the Band had been properly notified and had failed to file any written material justifying its position even after having been requested to do so by various officers of the Department; that after such a lengthy delay it would be unfair to Mr. Charlie to be faced with a further hear­ing; that the neglect exhibited by the Band and the Chief should not exonerate them from their primary obligations; and finally that the Adjudicator met the statutory re­quirements.
