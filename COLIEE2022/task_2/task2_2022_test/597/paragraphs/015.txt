The appellant and his wife arrived in Canada as landed immigrants on May 10, 1987. The appellant left the country for business reasons 18 days later on May 28, 1987. He returned in October, 1987 and spent 13 days in Canada on that occasion. His son and daughter arrived in Canada in June of 1987. While they return to Vancouver for holidays, they have been out of the country for most of the 1987-89 period pursuing their educations, either in Boston or elsewhere.
