  Decisions of the Immigration Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board dealing with questions of law are to be reviewed against a standard of correctness, whereas findings of fact are subject to the patent unreasonableness standard:  
 Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration) v. Thanabalasingham 
  (2004), 315 N.R. 91; 2004 FCA 4.
