  On March 20, 1996, the Defendants succeeded in obtaining an order prohibiting the Minister of Health from issuing a notice of compliance to Apotex in respect of the Canadian Patent No. 1,204,671 ('671 patent). The order was upheld on appeal: Hoffmann-La Roche Ltd. v. Canada(Minister of National Health and Welfare), [1996] F.C.J. No. 1334 (QL), (1996), 70 C.P.R. (3d) 1 (F.C.A.).
