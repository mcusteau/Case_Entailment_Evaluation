In addition to these considerations, s. 10 of the relevant Information Circular sets out a number of factors that will be considered when reaching a conclusion. Those factors are as follows: whether there is a history of compliance; whether the taxpayer knowingly allowed a balance to exist; whether the taxpayer has exercised a reasonable amount of care and has not been negligent; and, finally, whether the taxpayer has acted quickly to remedy any delay or omission.
