One 2 One Databased Marketing Communications Limited (hereinafter One 2 One) and MT&L Proactive Research Department Incorporated (hereinafter RDI) are companies carrying on polling and other forms of marketing research. The principals of RDI are Greg Trask and his wife Leslie Trask. Bill McArthur relinquished his interest in RDI in August 1997. Ian Thompson remains a minority shareholder only of RDI.
