KLJ Computer Solutions Incorporated (hereinafter KLJ) is a company providing computer services. The principals and only full-time employees of KLJ, in September 1997, were Kelli McGrath and her husband Jason McGrath. McArthur Thompson, having no particular computer expertise, relied on KLJ to be its computer consultants and technicians. KLJ occupied some space in McArthur Thompson's premises.
