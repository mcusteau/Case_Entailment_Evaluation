McArthur Thompson is a small respected Halifax advertising and public relations firm owned by William McArthur and Ian Thompson. Mr. McArthur founded the firm in 1976 as William F. McArthur and Associates and incorporated under that name in 1981. Ian Thompson became an active member of the company in 1987. The firm operates out of premises owned by its principals located at 1300 Hollis Street in Halifax. It has approximately 15 employees.
