The facts surrounding this dispute are substantially admitted and indeed the trial of the action proceeded on the basis of an Agreed Statement of Facts. This statement discloses that on or about May 29, 1980, the plaintiff exchanged some farm property with one Linda Armson. Both before and after the transfer, the plaintiff was engaged in farming operations. The transaction was at arm's length and no moneys were paid out or paid in. It was a simple exchange of properties of equal value.
