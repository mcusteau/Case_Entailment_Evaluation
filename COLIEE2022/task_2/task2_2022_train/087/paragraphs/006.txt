  Following review of her application, Ms. Saraffian was granted a pension under the  
 Old Age Security Act 
 , RSC 1985, c O-9 [OAS] of $358.79 per month, which was at that time 27/40ths of the full pension, as well as a Guaranteed Income Supplement of $583.22 per month.
