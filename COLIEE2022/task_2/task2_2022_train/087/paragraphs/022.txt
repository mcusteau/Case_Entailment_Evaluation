  The applicant submits that none of the cases relied upon by the Tribunal are pertinent to the facts of her case. In  
 Ata 
 , the applicant's residence in Canada did not count towards pensionable time because he was a diplomat from another country. In  
 Ding

 Perera 
 , the applicant voided his permanent residency by returning to his native Sri Lanka for three years.
