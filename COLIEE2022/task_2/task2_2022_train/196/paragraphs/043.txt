  Therefore, I am of the view that ss. 38 and 39 of the  
 VRAA 
  and the case law, when read together, require that contradictory evidence be adduced in the file before rejecting medical evidence adduced by the applicant. Unless the Board believed that the evidence was not credible, which was not the case here, it could not reject Dr. Sestier's opinion without having contradictory evidence before it.
