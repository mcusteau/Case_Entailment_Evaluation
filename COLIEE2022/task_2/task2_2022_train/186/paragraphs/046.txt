  I turn to the question of the impact which the delay had on the Defendants. The Defendants submit that the delay in the revocation proceedings caused them a great deal of uncertainty and distress; that it has deprived them of their ability to travel, which had an adverse effect on both their family life and Mr. Parekh's employment prospects; and that it has resulted in the treatment of their daughter's H&C application being put on hold.
