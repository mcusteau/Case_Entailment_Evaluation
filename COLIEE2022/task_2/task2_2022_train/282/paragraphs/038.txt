Erroneous findings of fact that are made in a "perverse or capricious manner or without regard to the material," have traditionally been reviewed on the patent unreasonableness standard:
Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration) v. Thanabalasingham
, [2004] 3 F.C.R. 523 (F.C.) at paragraph 51; Powell v. Canada (Minister of Human Resources Development), [2000] F.C.J. No. 1008 (F.C.A.); Mugesera v.
