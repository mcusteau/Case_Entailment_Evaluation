Mr Li cites concerns about the new policy, namely, that the increased priority given to cessation cases means that persons with only a fleeting connection with their countries of origin are being targeted and, potentially, removed from Canada. As mentioned, Mr Li's travels to and connections with his country of origin were extensive, and reasonably motivated the Minister's cessation application, as well as the Board's decision in the Minister's favour.
