On the matter of alleged retroactive application of a new policy, the evidence shows that the law on cessation of refugee status changed in 2012. Pursuant to the amendment, persons whose refugee status has ceased also lose their permanent residence status (
IRPA
, s 46(1)(
c
.1)). Along with that change came increased funding for cessation applications. In turn, cessation applications were given somewhat higher priority within the Canadian Border Services Agency.
