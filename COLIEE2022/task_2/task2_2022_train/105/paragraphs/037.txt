  With these factors in mind, the Court is of the view that the Crown Leases should be disclosed, but in a redacted form to ensure that the harm envisaged by the Applicant under s. 20(1)(d) of the  
 Act

 Act 
  require the terms of the Crown Leases to be disclosed in their entirety.
