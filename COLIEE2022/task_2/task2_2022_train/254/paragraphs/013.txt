Kwon is a citizen of Korea, married with two children, being a marketing manager for Dae Yong Metallic Industry. His I.M.M.8 letter requested that he be assessed as a "sales manager" and was filed with supporting documents and prescribed fee in Seoul on September 14, 1988. He indicated that he had a relative in Canada who was in a position to provide an "Undertaking of Assistance". At the time of his application, it is contended that he merited up to eight points under occupational demand for "manager, sales" and in any event should have been allocated a minimum of one point for this occupation.
