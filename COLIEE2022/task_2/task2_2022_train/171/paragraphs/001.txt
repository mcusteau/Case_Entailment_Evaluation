 Hughes, J. 
 : The Applicant, Pamela Egan, is an employee of the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA). She is legally blind and suffered a neck and back injury in 1998. She returned to work in 2001 and sought various forms of accommodation from her employer. The Applicant believed that her employer failed to make reasonable accommodation for her and, on May 21, 2003, filed a complaint with the Canadian Human Rights Commission.
