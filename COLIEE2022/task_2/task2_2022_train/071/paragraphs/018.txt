  On the other hand, the evidence regarding the possibility that Mr. Slaeman's son might have stolen the passports (set out in para 15 of Mr. Slaeman's Affidavit) and regarding the applicants' lack of knowledge of the impostors (set out in para 17 of Mr. Slaeman's Affidavit and in para 9 of Ms. Roukan's Affidavit) is not relevant to the applicants' procedural fairness claims. It is directed toward the merits of the inquiry before the adjudicator, namely, whether or not the applicants allowed the third-party impostors to use their passports. This evidence could have been - but was not - placed before the adjudicator.
