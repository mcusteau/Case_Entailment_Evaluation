  A finding of a valid IFA would generally be sufficient to dispose of a s. 97 claim. However, as I held in  
 Gnanasekaram v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration) 
 , 2007 FC 297, the Board errs when it makes no analysis of risk (in that case, as here, the risk in Colombo) where the evidence of danger is advanced and not addressed.
