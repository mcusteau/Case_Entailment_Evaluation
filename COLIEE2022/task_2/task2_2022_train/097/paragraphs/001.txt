 [1] 
   
 Campbell, J. 
 : In the present Application, the Applicant mounts a challenge, as an unrepresented litigant, to two decisions; a negative Pre-Removal Risk Assessment (PRRA) and a negative Humanitarian and Compassionate determination (H&C), both dated October 31, 2007. The decisions under review reject the joint pleas for relief of not only the Applicant, but also of his mother, father, and sister with respect to their prospective return to Kenya.
