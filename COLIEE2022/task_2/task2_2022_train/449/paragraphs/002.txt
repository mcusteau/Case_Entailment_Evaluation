The applicants are a husband, wife and their minor son. They are citizens of Guyana. They entered Canada as visitors along with their daughter on August 20, 2002 and made a refugee claim approximately a month later based on attacks against them and their business. The Refugee Protection Division (RPD) of the Immigration and Refugee Board rejected the applicants' refugee claims on March 23, 2003 on the basis that the attacks against them were not politically motivated and the risk did not persist. The applicants did not leave Canada.
