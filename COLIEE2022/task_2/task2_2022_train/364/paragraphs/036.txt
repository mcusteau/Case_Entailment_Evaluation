The most recent decision of the Federal Court of Appeal is
Mazda Canada Inc. v. Mitsui O.S.K. Lines Co. et al.
, [2009] 2 F.C.R. 382; 382 N.R. 378; 2008 FCA 219 (
The Cougar Ace
). That case concerned a shipment of automobiles from Japan intended for discharge in New Westminster, British Columbia.
The Cougar Ace
suffered a severe list during the voyage. The cargo was never actually delivered in Canada but rather was delivered in the United States.
