The panel noted certain inconsistencies in the applicant's evidence concerning the alleged events in 2000. It also reviewed the documentary evidence concerning Hezbollah activities and targets in Lebanon. On the basis of this evidence, the panel member concluded that "... the claimant's profile is dissimilar from that of persons in Lebanon who would be at risk of being threatened or harassed by members of Hizbollah for being suspected Israeli collaborators or spies."
