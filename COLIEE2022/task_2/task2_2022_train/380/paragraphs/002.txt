The relief requested is stated in the following terms in the Applicants' Originating Notice of Motion:
"(a) a writ of certiorari to quash the decision of Immigration Officers, wherein it was determined that there were no humanitarian or compassionate grounds to permit the Applicants to remain in Canada, such decision being communicated to the Applicants' counsel on April 16, 1992;
"(b) a writ of mandamus directing the Minister's Officials to consider the Applicants' request to remain in Canada in accordance with the duty of fairness and pursuant to the principal of consistent treatment under the Policy Guidelines."
