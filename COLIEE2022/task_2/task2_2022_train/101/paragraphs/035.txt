  In essence, the Guidelines ensure that appropriate consideration is given to factors that might not otherwise be given the weight they merit in the overall assessment of an Applicant's H&C application. However, it is still necessary to demonstrate that the application as a whole warrants the exceptional grant of discretion contemplated by section 25 of the IRPA, due to a likely risk of unusual and undeserved, or disproportionate, hardship, if the application is not granted.
