 O'Keefe, J. 
 : This is an application pursuant to subsection 72(1) of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act, S.C. 2001, c. 27 ("IRPA") for judicial review of the May 16, 2003 decision of a pre-removal risk assessment officer, (the "PRRA Officer"), wherein it was determined that Lajos Babai (the "applicant") would not be subject to a risk of persecution, torture, risk to life or risk of cruel and unusual treatment or punishment if returned to his country of nationality.
