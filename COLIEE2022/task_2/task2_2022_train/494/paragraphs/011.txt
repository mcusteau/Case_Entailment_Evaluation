In expungement proceedings, the trademark is presumed to be valid, and the onus lies on the party attacking the registration to prove, on a balance of probabilities, that the registration should be expunged. (
Uniwell Corp. v. Uniwell North America Inc.
(1996), 109 F.T.R. 81; 66 C.P.R.(3d) 436 at 438 (T.D.)).
