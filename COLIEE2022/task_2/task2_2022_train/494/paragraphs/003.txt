Since 1983, the applicant's main product has been the COMPULIFE Quotation System software, a life insurance analysis program which is sold to life insurance agents on a monthly subscription basis. The COMPULIFE Quotation System software is used to survey, compare, and analyze life insurance policies offered by various life insurance companies. In 1997, the applicant had approximately 800 subscribers to its service. Between 1983 and 1996, the applicant's advertizing expenditures totalled $240,573 and its sales totalled $6,430,730.
