I share the respondent's view that it is reasonable of the Board to have rejected the applicant's refugee claim, because it found his credibility was damaged with respect to his explanations about his relationship with Falun Gong, the crux of his claim. As Lemieux J. ruled in Neame,supra, the Board did not err in arriving at a negative finding on a crucial point of the applicant's claim and rejecting the refugee claim after the record was considered.
