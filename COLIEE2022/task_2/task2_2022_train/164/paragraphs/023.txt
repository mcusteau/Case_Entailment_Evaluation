  The Respondent argues that the Board was correct in its consideration of state protection. Specifically, the Board noted that, in the past year in Albania, only 2 murders out of 96 have been related to blood feuds. The Board also considered evidence that the government was enacting steep penalties for blood feud killings which were helping to decrease the practice. The Respondent contends that the Board's reasons demonstrate a full understanding of the documentary evidence.
