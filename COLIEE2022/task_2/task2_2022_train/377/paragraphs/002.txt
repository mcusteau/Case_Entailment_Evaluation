The applicant is a Sri Lankan Tamil from the Jaffna district. She arrived in Canada via Buffalo, New York on 17 July 1991 and claimed refugee status, which was denied by the CRDD on 24 March 1992. On 12 May 1992 she had a son in Canada. The son's father is a landed immigrant, but he cannot sponsor her because he is still married. He has been separated from his wife since he came to Canada in 1985 and does not know where she is. Divorce proceedings have been begun and he plans to marry the applicant as soon as he can.
