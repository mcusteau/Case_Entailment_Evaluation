  The Respondent adds that though this approach may appear at first glance harsh on visa applicants, it is necessary to ensure the administrative efficiency of a burdened system and to ensure finality of the decision-making process related to visa applications. To proceed otherwise would simply add delays to a processing system which is already very long. Fairness to all visa applicants requires that all applicants conform to the instructions they receive as to the type and quality of documentation required in support of their applications, thus ensuring a minimum of efficiency and equity in the system.
