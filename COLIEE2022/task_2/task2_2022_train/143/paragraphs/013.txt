  As for the second ground of review, the availability of risk to an Applicant upon return to their country of origin is a factor that is often assessed in the context of H&C applications. The way in which it is raised in the Officer’s reasons does not suggest that the Officer considered that the absence of risk in any way negated the Applicant’s claim. The Officer merely identified that the factor does not arise in this case for consideration.
