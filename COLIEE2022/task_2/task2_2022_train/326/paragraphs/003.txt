The current issues reflect deep political divisions within the I.T.U.M. Council. The considerable amount of evidence offered by the parties in the current case, as well as in related cases, reveals a great degree of tension and mistrust between the former and the new band chief and their respective supporters; although the reasons for these divisions are difficult to identify and have undoubtedly multiple causes, there is no doubt that personality conflicts played a major role here, along with the fact that some non-members may have been involved in the governance of the Band.
