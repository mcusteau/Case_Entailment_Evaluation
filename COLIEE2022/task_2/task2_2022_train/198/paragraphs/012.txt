  The Board also took issue with the Applicant's explanation that she had not read Zhuan Falun, a book written by Master Li Hongzhi. She claimed she did not understand it. The Board found this answer "strange," given the Applicant's eleven years of formal education (Decision at page 10). The Board also noted that several parts of the book contain lectures that are interesting and are not abstract, and found that the Applicant's answers eroded her credibility.
