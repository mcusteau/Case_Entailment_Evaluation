  The plaintiff alleges that Plasticair's conduct was improper, sometimes vexatious and significantly increased the amount of work that it had to carry out. It was required to file three Motions to compel the defendant to follow the rules and the time limit with respect to submissions of affidavit of documents. It contends that when finally four affidavits of documents were served, they included many lengthy and irrelevant documents. Moreover, when it requested that Plasticair identify the relevant documents as it was its duty to do, Plasticair failed to respond.
