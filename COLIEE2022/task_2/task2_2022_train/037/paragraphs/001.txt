 Gibson, J. 
 : These reasons follow the hearing of an application for judicial review of a decision of the Refugee Protection Division (the "RPD") of the Immigration and Refugee Board wherein the RPD determined the applicant not to be a Convention refugee or a person otherwise in need of Convention refugee-like protection in Canada. The decision under review is dated the 8th of May, 2007.
