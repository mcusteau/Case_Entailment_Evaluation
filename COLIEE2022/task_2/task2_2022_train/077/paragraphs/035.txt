  Based on the treatment homosexuals receive in Jamaica, as set out in the officer's decision, it is truly unfortunate if the Applicant is lesbian that she will be returned to Jamaica. However, every applicant for a Pre-removal Risk Assessment, and their counsel, must take responsibility to ensure that all of the relevant evidence is before the officer and, of equal importance, that they present the best evidence in support of the application. Where that is not done, the consequences of a failed application rest with the Applicant and counsel.
