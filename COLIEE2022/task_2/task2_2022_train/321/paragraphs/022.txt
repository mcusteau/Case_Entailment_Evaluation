Dealing first with the applicable standard of review, the pragmatic and functional analysis does not apply to judicial review brought on the ground of breach of procedural fairness. It is for the reviewing court to determine procedural fairness questions. See:
Canadian Union of Public Employees et al. v. Ontario (Minister of Labour)
, [2003] 1 S.C.R. 539; 304 N.R. 76; 173 O.A.C. 38; 2003 SCC 29, at paragraph 100.
