  However, in Pal v. Canada (M.C.I.), [2003] F.C.J. No. 894 (T.D.) (QL), as the jurisprudence was conflicting, Justice Simpson chose to follow Nagy v. Canada (M.C.I.), [2002] F.C.J. No. 370 (T.D.)
