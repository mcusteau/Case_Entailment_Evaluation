  In the final paragraph of its analysis, the Board wrote the following: 
 
 
 
 In the event that the panel does accept as credible the claimant's evidence of what occurred to her in 1992 her current fear in Haiti appears to be a fear of generalised violence. The claimant here could face a risk. However, it is a risk that is faced by the entire population of Haiti - including her mother. As a consequence the claimant cannot take the benefit of refugee protection.
