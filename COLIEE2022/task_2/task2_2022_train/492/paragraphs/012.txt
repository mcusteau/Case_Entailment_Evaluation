The applicant submits that kidnapping is rampant in Haiti, but businessmen are especially at risk because the goal of kidnapping for ransom is to obtain money. Given the fact that in Haiti most of the population is poor, those with money or those perceived to have money are at greater risk than the general population. Section 97 of the Act is to be interpreted as indicating that there can be a sub-group within a generalized group that faces an even more acute risk than the larger group.
