Since the applicant had a "valid and subsisting" passport at the time of his application, which is no longer disputed by the respondent, it was not within the officer's discretion to determine if the issuance of this document was satisfactory. The "satisfactory" test applies only to identity documents other than passports and if a person has a "valid and subsisting" passport or travel document, then the person will have satisfied the identity part of the requirement to become landed
