The visa officer's comments disclose that the respondent applies different stan­dards in the determination of satisfactory identification in cases such as this, in differ­ent circumstances. I find it of interest to note that, in Islamabad, much closer to the reality of the situation in Afghanistan and Pakistan, the principal applicant's documentation was found to be much above the average for persons fleeing Afghanistan.
