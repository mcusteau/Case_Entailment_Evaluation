  The Board also relied on the Respondent's testimony and correspondence between the Respondent's counsel and German consular officials in Canada as evidence of the content and meaning of section 44(1)2. The Board accepted this evidence as sufficient proof that under German law competent authorities would consider the Respondent to have relinquished her residency status in Germany upon her arrival in Canada because the Respondent had intended to leave Germany for good.
