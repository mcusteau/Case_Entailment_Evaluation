  The Respondent (now known as Melisa Joline Choubak) was born on June 11, 1982 in Tehran, Iran. She is a citizen of Iran. In March 1986, the Respondent accompanied her mother to Germany and was given asylum under the guardianship of her aunt and uncle by a judgment of the local guardianship court. Her aunt and uncle had been accepted in Germany as refugees from Iran in 1985. The Respondent was granted a special temporary residence permit. Her mother travelled back to Iran, and visited the Respondent once a year in Germany when she was allowed to leave Iran. In 1998, the Respondent's mother moved to Germany and reclaimed guardianship over the Respondent.
