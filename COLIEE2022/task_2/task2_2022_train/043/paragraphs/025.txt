  Section 18 of the  
 Veterans Review and Appeal Board Act 
  gives the VRAB "full and exclusive jurisdiction to hear, determine and deal with all applications for review that may be made to the [VRAB] under the  
 Pension Act 
 ". That  
 Act 
  also has a privative clause in section 31, which states that a "decision of the majority of members of an appeal panel is a decision of the [VRAB] and is final and binding".
