In addition, while the NOC is incorporated by reference into the
Immigration Regulations, 1978
, SOR/78-172 by s. 8(1) and Schedule I, it was not drafted with a view to determining legal rights and duties, and is accordingly not written in the style of a legal instrument. Consequently, when subjected to the searchlight of legal analysis, its provisions will often be found to be ambiguous or vague, as is the case here.
