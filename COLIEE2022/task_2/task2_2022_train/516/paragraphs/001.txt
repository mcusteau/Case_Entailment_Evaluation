Simpson, J.
: The Applicant has brought an application for leave and judicial review of a decision of a removals officer dated October 28, 2003, (the "Decision"). In that Decision, the officer refused to defer the Applicant's removal, which had been scheduled for November 15, 2003. In this context, the Applicant has moved for a stay of his removal pending the disposition of his outstanding application for judicial review and his outstanding application for landing from within Canada on humanitarian and compassionate grounds (the "H & C Application").
