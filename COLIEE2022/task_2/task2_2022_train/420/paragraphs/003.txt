On September 25, 1990, in an appeal by Pick-a-Mix Concrete Limited, the Canadian International Trade Tribunal ("CITT") decided that sales tax on concrete mixed at the job site should be calculated after deducting transportation costs:
Pick-A-Mix Concrete Ltd. v. Minister of National Revenue
(1990), 3 T.C.T. 2347 (C.I.T.T.).
