  In  
 Inspiration Management Ltd. v. McDermid St. Lawrence Ltd. 
  (1989), 36 B.C.L.R. (2d) 202, the British Columbia Court of Appeal set out a number of factors a judge should consider in determining whether a summary trial is appropriate: 
 
 
 
 • amount involved; 
 
 
 
 • complexity of the matter; 
 
 
 
 • cost of a conventional trial relative to amount claimed; and 
 
 
 
 • course of proceedings.
