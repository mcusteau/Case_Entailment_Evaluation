  Were it not for an error made by the visa officer who initially reviewed Ms. Kaur Gill's sponsorship application, that application likely would have been successful. However, between the time the sponsorship application was rejected by the visa officer and rejected again by the Immigration Appeal Division [IAD] of the Refugee Board of Canada, the legal test applicable to such applications changed. Unfortunately, her counsel has not been able to identify any principle of law upon which the Court can rely to keep alive her hope of sponsoring her husband for permanent residence in Canada.
