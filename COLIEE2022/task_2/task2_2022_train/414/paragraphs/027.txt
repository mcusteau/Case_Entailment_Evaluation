A few words, however, must be said in regard to an argument made by the applicant to counter the respondent's submission that I should follow the Court of Appeal's decision in
Anderson
. The applicant brought to my attention art. 19.26(16) of the
Q.R.&O.
which provides that:
"(16) A redress authority in receipt of a complaint of an officer or noncommissioned member under this article shall suspend any action in respect of the complaint where the member initiates an action, claim or complaint under an Act of Parliament, other than the National Defence Act, in respect of the matter giving rise to the member's complaint under this article."
