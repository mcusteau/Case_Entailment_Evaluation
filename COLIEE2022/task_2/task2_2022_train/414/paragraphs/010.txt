Pursuant to s. 19.26(2), a member of the Armed Forces, must submit his complaint "through the chain of command". That is, he must submit his complaint first to his commanding officer, then if not satisfied with the decision rendered,
to the Chief of the Defence Staff, and finally if not satisfied with the decision of the Chief of the Defence Staff, the member may then submit his complaint to the Minister of National Defence. In the present matter, the applicant did not submit his complaint to the Chief of the Defence Staff nor to the Minister of National Defence.
