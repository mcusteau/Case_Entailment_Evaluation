 Tremblay-Lamer, J. 
 : This is an application pursuant to subsection 72(1) of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act, S.C. c. 27 (the "Act"), for judicial review of a decision of the Refugeee Protection Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board (the "RPD"), wherein the RPD determined that the applicant was not a Convention refugee or person in need of protection according to sections 96 and 97 of the Act, respectively.
