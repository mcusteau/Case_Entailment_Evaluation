  The applicants made further submissions to the Officer relating primarily to the risk factors. They submitted that they would face undue, disproportionate and undeserved hardships if they were required to apply for permanent resident status from outside of Canada. They submitted that they are at risk because of the discrimination they face as South Asians living in Kenya, and because they are targets of criminals because of their ethnicity and gender. They also argued that they should be granted an exemption on H&C grounds because of their high degree of establishment in Canada.
