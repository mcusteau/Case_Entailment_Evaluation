Daiichi is also the owner of Canadian Patent 1, 157, 840 (the "'840 patent"), issued on May 22, 1984, which disclosed and claimed an antibiotic known as "ofloxacin". Janssen-Ortho was also licensed by Daiichi to market ofloxacin in Canada, which it did under the brand name FLOXIN. The '840 ofloxacin patent expired on May 22, 2001.
