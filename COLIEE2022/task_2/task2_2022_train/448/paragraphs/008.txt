Levofloxacin, the subject of the '080 patent and these proceedings, and ofloxacin, disclosed in the expired '840 patent, can both be described as broad-spectrum, synthetic antibacterial agents useful in the treatment of infections caused by certain strains of microorganisms, such as certain types of pneumonia, influenza, skin infections and urinary tract infections. Ofloxacin and levofloxacin formulas have also been marketed for eye infections under different brand names.
