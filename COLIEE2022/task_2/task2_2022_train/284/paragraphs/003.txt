The plaintiff, Joen Pauli Rasmussen (hereinafter: Capt. Rasmussen) is a resident of the Faroe Islands. At all material times he was the master of the "Bordoyarnes" (hereinafter: the fishing vessel, the vessel, or, the ship) a 45.1 m long, 181 tons, steel-hulled longliner which was owned by S/LF Bordoyarvik, a body corporate under the laws of the Faroe Islands, with head office at Klakksvik therein. The corporate plaintiff was previously known as S/LF Boreas.
