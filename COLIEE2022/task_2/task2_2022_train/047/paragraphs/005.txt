  In 1999, the TSB conducted a closed competition to staff on an indeterminate basis a Data Analyst - Air position (competition number 99-TSB-HO-CCID-073). The applicant had previously held the position for one year on a term basis. Candidates were required to undergo a written examination which assessed a candidate's knowledge and his or her ability to communicate in writing. The applicant passed the knowledge section of the test, but failed the abilities part of the test. The respondent Mr. Martin Tremblay was selected as the successful candidate.
