  To answer the first question, I find that the CBSA report is an instrument of advocacy. The CBSA report discloses a number of serious allegations in considerable detail. The allegations appear to tie the Applicant or his publishing company to known organized crime figures. In cross-examination on his affidavit, the Officer was asked about his reliance on the CBSA report. He made a telling admission: "I did consider it, but I did not list it in the procedural fairness letter".
