Cullen, J.
: This is an application for judicial review of a decision of the Canada Immigration Centre, Toronto Refugee Backlog Office, dated April 14, 1993, refusing the applicant's application for permanent residence, pursuant to the
Refugee Claimants Designated Class Regulations
SOR/90-40 (am. SOR/92-13) because he was considered to be criminally inadmissible. The applicant is requesting that the decision be quashed and that this court direct that his application for permanent residence should be processed in accordance with s. 19(2)(a) of the
Immigration Act
, R.S.C. 1985, c. I-2 (as amended to S.C. 1992, c. 1) ("the
Act
") as it stood prior to February 1, 1993.
