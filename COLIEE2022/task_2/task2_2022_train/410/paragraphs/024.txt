The applicant asserts that he was informed by a representative of the Department that his application was complete save for the receipt of the confirmation of his summary conviction. Thus, he was left with the impression that this last step was merely a formality and that he had been accepted as landed. The respondent denies this and argues that it was unclear whether a summary conviction would have been a bar to landing under the old provision and therefore the process could not possibly have been complete. With respect, I cannot agree.
