The Applicant is a corporation registered under the laws of Ontario; it began operation in 1983. Since its inception, the Applicant has manufactured and sold a range of frozen bakery products, namely, muffin, cake and cookie batters, croissants, danishes and other pastries. In 1990, the Applicant began selling fresh baked goods as well. The Applicant's wares are sold in association with the trademark TRADITION to various food distributors, who in turn sell the products to retail outlets such as grocery stores, convenience stores, donut and muffin shops, and restaurants. The Applicant also sells its products to institutional purchasers such as hotels.
