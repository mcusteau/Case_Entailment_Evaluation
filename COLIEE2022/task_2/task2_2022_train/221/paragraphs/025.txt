  The Applicants alleged that the Officer violated the duty of fairness by relying on the Pre-Removal Risk Assessment conducted in February 2005, despite submissions made by counsel that this risk assessment should be disregarded since it relied on extrinsic evidence which had not been provided to the Applicants for review and comment. This extrinsic evidence consisted of a report on Argentina prepared by Latin Focus, according to which Argentina's economy was improving.
