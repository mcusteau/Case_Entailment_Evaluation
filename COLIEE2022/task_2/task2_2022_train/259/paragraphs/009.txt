Apotex raises issues as to the nature and extent of the remedy afforded by section 8 of the
PMNOC Regulations
, in particular:
1. Is Apotex entitled to an election as between the damages which it has suffered, if any, and the profits made by Merck, if any?
2. What is the period of time in respect of which Apotex may claim recovery?
3. Is Apotex entitled to recover for damages that continue after the period expires?
