Section 11(1) of the
Patent Amendment Act
1992, S.C. 1993, c. 2, provides as follows:
"11(1) A licence that has been granted under s. 39 of the former Act before December 20, 1991 (as is the fact here), and that has not been terminated before the commencement day shall continue in effect according to its terms and, subject to subs. (2), ss. 39 to 39.14 of the former Act shall continue to apply in respect of that licence as if they had not been repealed by s. 3 of this Act."
