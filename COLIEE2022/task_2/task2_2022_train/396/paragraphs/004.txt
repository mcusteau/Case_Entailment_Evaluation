By letter dated 26 October 1995, the applicant was informed of the possible issuance of a Ministerial Opinion pursuant to s. 70(5) of the

The opinion that ensued, that the respondent was of the opinion that the applicant constituted a danger to the public in Canada, was dated 29 December 1995. A warrant for the applicant's arrest was executed on 4 January 1996, and he was detained.
