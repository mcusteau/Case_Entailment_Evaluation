  In fact, these very issues were raised by the Board itself at the outset of the hearing (Tribunal Record, page 103). The first has to do with Mr. Shwaba's credibility. This Court has stated on a number of occasions that whether a claimant has established a well-founded fear of persecution is a question of mixed fact and law, and calls for a careful analysis of the claimant's testimony and of the documentary evidence about country conditions. Here, we have none of the two.
