  Counsel for the respondent also pointed to the fact she had completed her Schedule 1 information form for the purpose of ascertaining her eligibility to her refugee claim filed in Canada, which seems to have been accepted by the Minister. She argued that in all of the circumstances it was not premature to consider alternatives for her release as Member Rempel had decided on the September 14, 2010 review.
