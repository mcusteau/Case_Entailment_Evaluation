  The evidence relating to the accident was provided in a letter to the applicant. It indicated that: (1) the man who purchased the applicant's car died in a car accident involving that car; (2) the circumstances surrounding the accident were very suspicious; and (3) the police asked the wife of the dead man if there had been a woman or a little boy, fitting the applicant's description, with him at the time of the accident.
