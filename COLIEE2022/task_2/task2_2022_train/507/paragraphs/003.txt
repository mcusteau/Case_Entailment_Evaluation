The applicant served in the Armed Forces Primary Reserve from November 24, 1983 to December 6, 1984. He was a member of the Cameron Highlanders of Ottawa regiment. During winter training in 1983-1984 he fell through the surface of deep snow into a crevasse. He was carrying a heavy radio backpack secured with a waistband and headband. He states that the fall caused his backpack to shift and the headband snapped his neck backward and sideways resulting in a whiplash type injury.
