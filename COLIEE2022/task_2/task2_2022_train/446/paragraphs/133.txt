In
Thamotharem
, Justice Blanchard determined that the implementation of Guideline 7 had the effect of fettering a Board member's discretion to decide the most appropriate process to follow in the circumstances of each case. Fundamental to the right of a fair hearing is that a Board member exercise independent judgment in deciding a case on its merits free from undue influence. Such fettering, in Justice Blanchard's view, constitutes undue influence upon the member and violates the principles of procedural fairness.
