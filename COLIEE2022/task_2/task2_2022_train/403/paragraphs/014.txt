The documents in question are a letter dated July 14, 1998 and a five page RCMP document which includes form A-159. This latter document, form A-159, was signed by the applicant upon his discharge from the RCMP. In it, the applicant was asked to provide the name and date of injuries or diseases resulting in disability for which a pension is claimed. The applicant answered that he received a back injury during recruit training in 1964, and he attributed it to "equitation training."
