The plaintiff has testified that on the morning of October 18, 1985, he again attended at the station to pick up the garbage. The decal, which he had been unable to remove, was covered up with masking tape. He states that he spoke with Sergeant Stanley, who informed him that he could not enter the station until the decal was removed from the windshield. The plaintiff states that accordingly, he was unable to enter the station to pick up garbage on that day.
