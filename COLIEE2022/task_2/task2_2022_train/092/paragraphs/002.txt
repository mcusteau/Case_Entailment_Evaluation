  Sehlule Nyathi is a 32-year old citizen of Zimbabwe. As a member of the Movement For Democratic Change ("MDC"), she claims a well-founded fear of persecution from the ruling Zimbabwe African National Union - Patriotic Front ("ZANU-PF"), on the basis of her political opinion. She also claims to be a person in need of protection pursuant to s. 97 of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act, S.C. 2001, c. 27 (the "Act").
