Her mother, allegedly, was arrested and imprisoned in Luanda. Ms. Augusto visited her twice before she was transferred to another facility. The police, later, questioned Ms. Augusto about the missing documents and her mother's political activities. Fearing that she would suffer the same fate as her cousin, she claims that she went to live with her mother's boyfriend and he facilitated her departure to Canada. She says that before she left Angola, a neighbour informed her that the Luanda police had come to her mother's house looking for her.
