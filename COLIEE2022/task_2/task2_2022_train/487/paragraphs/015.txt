I should also add that as a matter of fairness the record should show that the applicant was given the opportunity to provide information in support of his current experience in each included occupation. The record must equally indicate reasons which support the visa officer's assignment of a specific experience rating to the included occupations or reasons which support the refusal to do so. Obviously, having erroneously concluded that no assessment need be done, the visa officer in this case failed in this aspect of the duty of fairness.
