  Use of the word "shall" in subsection 28(2) of the  
 OASA 
  indicates that the Review Tribunal's jurisdiction is not permissive or discretionary. An appeal concerning "income"  
 shall 
  be referred for adjudication to the Tax Court of Canada. How then is "income" defined? Section 2 of the  
 OASA 
  states: 
 
 
 
 "'income' of a person for a calendar year means the person's income for the year, computed in accordance with the Income Tax Act ..."
