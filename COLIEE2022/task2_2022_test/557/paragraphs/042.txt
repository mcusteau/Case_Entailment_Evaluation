The tribunal's analysis that the applicant is safe because other Bulgarians would not recognize him as being Roma is incomplete. In this instance, it was the authorities which were the source of the applicant's problems. That they have already determined that he is Roma belies the tribunal's assumption that his risk is a mere possibility. That they already know that he is a Roma would have been sufficient reason for the tribunal to consider his risk at their authoritarian hands as well as at the hands of other Bulgarians.
