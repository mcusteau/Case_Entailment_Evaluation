The Applicant alleges that goons associated with the Bangladesh National Party (BNP) targeted him because of his activities on behalf of the BCL and, in particular, because of his help in printing and disseminating posters and leaflets condemning the activities of the BNP. The posters were created during the period leading up to the national election held in October 2001, and the leaflets were produced following the victory of a coalition of parties which included the BNP.
