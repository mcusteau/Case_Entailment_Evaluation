Jerome, A.C.J.
: This is an appeal by the defendant from the decision of the Associate Senior Prothonotary dated April 6, 1992, requiring the defendant to provide further and better particulars with respect to certain allegations contained in its Particulars of Objection. The defendant claims the plaintiffs' patent to be invalid on the grounds that the invention described is not novel, is obvious and does not involve any inventive step.
