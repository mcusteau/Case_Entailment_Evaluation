If the letter of August 10 were the only communication between the parties in regard to extension or renewal of the permit, the applicant's claim that the process was unfair might have more merit. But it was not. There were two prior meetings of the applicant with Ms. Jaworski, the immigration officer principally concerned, on July 22 and July 31. The evidence of what transpired at those meetings differs, in the versions set out by Ms. Jaworski in her affidavit and that set out by the applicant in his affidavit, and in his examination in relation to his affidavit and because of the different versions, in relation to that set out by Ms. Jaworski's affidavit.
