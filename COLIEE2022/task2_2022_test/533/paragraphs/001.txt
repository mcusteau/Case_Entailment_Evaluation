MacKay, J.
: This is an application for judicial review pursuant to s. 18.1 of the
Federal Court Act
, R.S.C. 1985, c. F-7 as amended. The applicant seeks an order to quash the decision of R. Gushuliak, made on behalf of the respondent, determining that a Minister's permit which was issued to the applicant would not be extended and further requesting the applicant to depart from Canada, and for an order prohibiting the respondent from proceeding with enforcement action against the applicant.
