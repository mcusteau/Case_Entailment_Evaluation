Mr. Wiebe and Mr. Perfanick then proceeded to assemble all of the equipment required by the contract to be provided by Arctic Rentals, and the additional equipment together with that already near Jenpeg was moved to Oxford House in early April, arriving there about April 10th or 11th. The equipment so moved is specified in the agreed statement of facts. It is also agreed that this equipment was not "fully demobilized" (i.e. removed from Oxford House to an accessible site) and repaired until February, 1979.
