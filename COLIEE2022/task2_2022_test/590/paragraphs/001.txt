Strayer, J.
: This is an action for breach of an oral contract in which the plaintiff claims $294,957.73 as the amount owing under the alleged contract, together with prejudgment interest at the rate of one and one-half percent per month on that amount from June 1, 1978 until the date of judgment. As it emerged in the evidence, the plaintiff asserts that this alleged oral contract was made in a telephone conversation between William Wiebe, President of the plaintiff, and Jeff Keto, Acting-Manager of the Canada Manpower Centre in Thompson, Manitoba, on March 20, 1978.
