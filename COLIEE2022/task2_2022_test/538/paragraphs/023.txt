Essentially, Mr. Zheng disagrees with the RPD’s weighing of the evidence.  As stated earlier, that is the responsibility of the RPD (Eker v Canada (Citizenship and Immigration), 2015 FC 1226 at para 9). The RPD has vast experience in weighing country condition evidence, including claims involving China and its exit controls. The RPD, not this Court, possesses the expertise in such matters. Its conclusions are owed deference: Gong v Canada (Citizenship and Immigration), 2017 FC 165 at paras 14-18.
