On June 8, 2005, the applicant allegedly took part in a CUD demonstration protesting the results of the 2005 Ethiopian election. Based on his participation in this demonstration, the applicant states that he was arrested and detained by authorities from June 10, 2005 until July 24, 2005, a period of over six weeks. While detained, the applicant maintains that he was interrogated about his support for the CUD, as well as his criticism of the government's agricultural policy. The applicant further states that during this time he was "beaten, flogged, slapped, and hit with a baton and rifle."
