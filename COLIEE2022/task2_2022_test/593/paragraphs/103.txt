I would also like to make it quite clear that any seamen's claims for gross wages should be reduced by the amount of their contribution, as such sums are part of the maritime liens claimed by N.A.T. Furthermore, any repatriation expenses or additional salaries already paid by N.A.T. to or for the benefit of seamen must be deducted from the claims made by the seamen and made part of N.A.T.'s claim.
