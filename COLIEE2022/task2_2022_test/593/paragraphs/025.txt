Although these three cases would appear to have definitively addressed the treatment of foreign maritime liens in Canadian courts, counsel for Baseline Industries Ltd. has launched an attack on N.A.T.'s entitlement to the relief it claims on three grounds:
I. The rights accorded to N.A.T. in Greek law do not amount to a maritime lien;
II. Canadian jurisprudence supports either a distinction or a deviation from the principle in The "Ioannis Daskelelis";
III. The Federal Court does not have jurisdiction to hear and adjudicate any claim inter partes that is not a basic maritime claim.
