The applicant submits that while the decision to hold a hearing is discretionary, the Officer nonetheless had an obligation to consider the request for an oral hearing, with reference to the factors set out in section 167 of the Regulations. The respondent submits that an oral hearing was not required because the Officer did not make a credibility finding against the applicant. Therefore, it is of no consequence that the Officer failed to refer specifically to the applicant's request for an oral hearing or to the above factors.
