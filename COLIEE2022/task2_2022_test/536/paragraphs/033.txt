Using the applicant's rationale, if every Band Council member failed to show up to three consecutively scheduled meetings, they would all be guilty of vacating their positions. The respondents submit that the applicant is incorrectly using the terms duly constituted and duly scheduled interchangeably. I agree with the respondents' assertion that the applicant is wrong in believing that just because a meeting is scheduled it is therefore a duly constituted meeting.
