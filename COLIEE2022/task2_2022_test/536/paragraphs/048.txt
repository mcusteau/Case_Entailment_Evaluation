"Furthermore, as members of an elected body engaged in purely legislative decision-making, there is nothing to prevent the defendant Councillors from holding meetings among themselves to discuss issues concerning the Band. The courts have recognized that advance discussion by elected members of government in matters coming forward for decision will be inevitable:
Old St. Boniface Residents Assn. Inc. v. Winnipeg (City)
