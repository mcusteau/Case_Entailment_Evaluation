Also, it would seem that the frequency of special meetings, outlined in section 11.4 of the NHCN
Guidelines
, have replaced the frequency of regular scheduled meetings. This in and of itself is contrary to the spirit of the rules. Special meetings cannot become the norm. Further, all meetings should be held in public and ordinary members of the Band should be made aware of the rules and the frequency of the meetings. If a meeting is cancelled, notice should be provided in order to inform members of the Band when a replacement meeting will be held.
