Justice Romilly recognized that Band Council decisions were binding if derived from powers conferred by the
Act
, reached by a majority vote at a duly convened meeting and not made in bad faith. Acting in accordance with the rule of law entails the obligation to adhere to the notion of democracy and a commitment to respect the duty of procedural fairness regarding decisions Band councillors take in the interest of those they were elected to protect.
