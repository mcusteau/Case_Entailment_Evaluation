The visa officer concluded that Ms. Turcinovica did not meet the definition on the basis that she would "in effect' be an employee of a company working on commission. While the visa officer understood Ms. Turcinovica's intent to be to work on commission for a number of companies selling investment vehicles, the visa officer refused the application on the basis that "[y]ou do not have any intention of establishing or purchasing a business, but rather you intend to work as an employee of an insurance company and work on a commission basis".
