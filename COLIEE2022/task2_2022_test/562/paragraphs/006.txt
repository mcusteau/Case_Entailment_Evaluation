Although he had not seen the Security Intelligence Report itself, Mr. Brown was nevertheless of the view that the information contained in the Report was not accurate, up to date or complete, and that it should therefore be removed from his file. To this end, he filed a series of grievances, which culminated in a March 18, 2005 decision rendered by a Commissioner's delegate. The Commissioner's delegate allowed Mr. Brown's grievance, in part.
