Heneghan, J.
: Ms. Armida Esparza-Alvarez and her daughter Elizabeth Armida Perez-Esparaza (the "Applicants") seek judicial review of the decision dated August 10, 2006, of the Immigration and Refugee Board, Refugee Determination Division (the "Board"). In its decision, the Board determined that the Applicants are not Convention refugees nor persons in need of protection as defined in sections 96 and 97, respectively, of the
Immigration and Refugee Protection Act
, S.C. 2001, c. 27, as amended (the "Act").
