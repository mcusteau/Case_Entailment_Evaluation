  The Board accepted the applicant's identity. The Board described the applicant's contention that he fears the current government of Sierra Leone because of his involvement with the AFRC government as being central to his claim, noting that he had testified that he risks being put to death like many of his colleagues. The Board also noted the applicant's argument that he had been forced to become involved with the AFRC, but found that a different picture emerged through his testimony and from the documentary evidence.
