  The applicant states that the documentary evidence is inconsistent with the Board's finding that "Guatemala has a functioning political and judicial system" and "an official apparatus that provides a measure of protection to its citizens including a criminal justice system and a functioning police force". The applicant contends that Guatemala is simply unable to protect its population from the maras, and that placing too heavy a burden on the applicants to rebut the presumption of state protection is inappropriate.
