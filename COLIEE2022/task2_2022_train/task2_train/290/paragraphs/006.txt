The judgment creditor argues that neither of these cases apply to the facts in this situation because the judgment creditor is not claiming relief against the Crown. Rather, he is simply invoking a Federal Court procedure for enforcing an order that has already been obtained pursuant to the decree nisi. In the same vein, the judgment creditor also submits that "impleaded" as used by Chief Jacket in Canadian Javelin Limited means "to prosecute or to sue"; and since the Crown in right of Ontario is neither being sued nor prosecuted, the garnishment rules provided for in Federal Court Rules may be invoked.
