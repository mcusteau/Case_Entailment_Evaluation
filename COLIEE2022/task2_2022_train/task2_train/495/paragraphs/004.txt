The appropriate burden of proof is set out in
Rasaratnam v. Minister of Employment and Immigration
, [1992] 1 F.C. 706; 140 N.R. 138 (F.C.A.), at pp. 708-710: "[t]he Board must be satisfied
on a balance of probabilities
that there is
no serious possibility of the claimant being persecuted
in the part of the country to which it finds an IFA exists."
