  The Board erred in making this plausibility finding without adequate regard to the applicant's age, culture, background and prior experiences:  
 Cooper v Canada (Citizenship and Immigration) 
 , 2012 FC 118, para 4. As set out in the Gender Guidelines, a claimant's steps in seeking state protection must be assessed with regard to "the social, cultural, religious, and economic context in which the claimant finds herself."
