Counsel for the respondent argues that the Board's statement that Dr. Lovell's opinion was not supported by x-ray documentation must be read in the context of the rest of the text of that decision and the previous Board decisions, and that the Board was referring to the lack of x-rays from 1983-1984. Counsel's written argument reads:
"It is clear from the Appeal Panel's reasons that it is not stating that no x-ray evidence was presented. What the Appeal Panel found was that there was no x-ray documenting that the origin of the injury occurred in 1983/1984. Indeed, the applicant's evidence was that x-rays were not taken until many years after his discharge from the forces ..."
