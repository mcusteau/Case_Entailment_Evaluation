  Thus the Defendants' applications for Canadian passports were being denied  
 even as no action was being taken to revoke their citizenship 
 . The documents put in evidence at trial and the testimony of Ms. Demers establish that Passport Canada communicated with CIC and inquired about the Defendants' citizenship status. CIC advised Passport Canada that they intended to proceed with the revocation of the Defendants' citizenship. It is a reasonable inference for the Court to find that Passport Canada's position was a direct consequence of CIC's advice.
