Gibson, J.
: These reasons arise out of a reference to the court made by the applicant pursuant to s. 18 of the
Citizenship Act
, R.S.C. 1985, c. C-29 (as amended) and rule 920 of the
Federal Court Rules
, C.R.C. 1978, c. 663. The applicant seeks a declaration that the respondent obtained Canadian citizenship by false representations or by knowingly concealing material circumstances. The evidence before me discloses that the principal allegation relates to the respondent allegedly knowingly concealing material circumstances in his application for Canadian citizenship. The Notice of Reference was filed the 30th of November, 1995.
