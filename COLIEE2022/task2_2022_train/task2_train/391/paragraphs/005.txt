The Defendant, Edward Sivitilli, filed a statement of defence which denied the allegations or claimed to have no knowledge of the facts. The Defendant asserted: that any damages suffered by the Plaintiffs were not caused by the Defendant's actions; that any damages sustained are excessive and too remote to be recoverable; and, that the Plaintiff did not hold registered trade-marks for the products. The Defendant did not adduce or submit any evidence and did not cross-examine any of the affiants.
