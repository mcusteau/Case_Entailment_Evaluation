  At the time of the visa officer's decision, section 4 of the  
 Immigration and Refugee Protection Regulations 
 , SOR/2002-2007 [Regulations] set forth a conjunctive test which provided that a foreign national could not be considered to be a spouse within the meaning of the Regulations if the marriage in question was (i) not genuine,  
 and 
  (ii) was entered into primarily for the purpose of acquiring any status or privilege under the IRPA.
