  Further, the applicant claims that her employment situation was effected because of her refusal to help the police in their search for her husband. In March 1990, the applicant was transferred to another hospital which was further away from her home. Her new supervisor was over-vigilant in watching for mistakes. She was told that if she made one mistake she would be fired. In the spring of 1990 the applicant along with other employees at the hospital participated in a one-day strike to protest against the government. There is no indication that any retaliation came out of this.
