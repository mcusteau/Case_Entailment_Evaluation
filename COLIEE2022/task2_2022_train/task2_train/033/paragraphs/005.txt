  Nor-Equip applied for the 469 Patent on October 25, 1989. The named inventor, Pertii Raty, was employed as a consultant by Nor-Equip. He was paid for his work. Mr. Raty assigned his interest in the 469 Patent to Nor-Equip. The 469 Patent was issued on May 17, 1994 in the name of Walter DiTullio, pursuant to a back-dated assignment agreement filed in the Patent Office in 1993. DeTullio signed on behalf of both the assignor and assignee.
