  A policy letter form the CRA, also referred to in the reasons for decision as the "memo from head office" addressed to all Revenue Collection Officers, stresses the need for a uniform application of the policy, and states that "[n] either ignorance of the legislation nor third party errors are acceptable reasons for the waiver/cancellation of penalty and interest under the Fairness provisions, and do not constitute extraordinary circumstances which warrant granting fairness relief."
