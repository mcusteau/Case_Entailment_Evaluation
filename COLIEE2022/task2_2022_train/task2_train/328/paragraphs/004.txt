At the credible basis hearing which took place on March 9, 1992, the adjudicator, after asking the applicant if he required an interpreter and the applicant saying that he needed one, proceeded to conduct an inquiry to determine if the applicant could understand and communicate in English. The adjudicator asked the applicant about his schooling, his employment experience, how he got his Ontario driver's licence and how he communicated with his counsel and at work in Canada.
