  Had the Board member accepted that a risk of rape is grounded in the applicant's membership in a particular social group - albeit a risk generally faced by members of that social group - then the inquiry should have resulted in a determination of whether there is "more than a mere possibility" that the applicant risks suffering this harm in Haiti. Inherent in this next step of the analysis is a determination as to whether adequate state protection is available to the applicant.
