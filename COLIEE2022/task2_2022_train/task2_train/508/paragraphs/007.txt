As stated earlier Nu-Pharm delivered to Pfizer and to the Minister, a Notice of Allegation stating that no claim for the medicine itself and no claim for the use of the medicine in Patent No. 1,181,076 (the "Pfizer patent") would be infringed by the making, constructing, using or selling fluconazole tablets by Nu-Pharm. In addition, Nu-Pharm undertook not to use or sell fluconazole made by a process covered by the Pfizer patent until after the expiry of the Pfizer patent.
