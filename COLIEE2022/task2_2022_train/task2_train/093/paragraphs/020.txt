  The applicant alleges that the panel circumvented the applicable principles of justice and disregarded the evidence dealing with the respondent's exclusion. The applicant argues that the panel erred in concluding that the respondent had no knowledge of the atrocities committed by the MLC and did not share its intent. Moreover, the applicant criticizes the panel for entertaining the notion that the goal sought by the MLC justifies the illicit means it employed to achieve its ends.
