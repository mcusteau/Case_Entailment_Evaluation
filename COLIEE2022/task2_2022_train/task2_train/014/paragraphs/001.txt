 de Montigny, J. 
  [Translation]: This is an application for judicial review of a decision of the Refugee Protection Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board (the Board) dated June 9, 2004, allowing the Minister's application to vacate the applicant's refugee status. Relying on subsection 109(1) of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act (the Act), the Board found that the applicant had obtained refugee status by misrepresenting a material fact relating to a relevant matter.
