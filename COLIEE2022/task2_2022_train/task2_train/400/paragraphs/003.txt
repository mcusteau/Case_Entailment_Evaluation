The applicant is a citizen of the Philippines and has been a resident of the Crown Colony of Hong Kong since March 1984. The applicant was initially employed in Hong Kong by Mr. Victor Leung and his family as a domestic worker from March 1984 to the end of March 1986 by virtue of an employment contract dated November 16, 1983 between Mr. Leung as "employer" and the applicant as "helper". By an agreement dated January 15, 1986 the employment contract was renewed for a further period of two years from March 30, 1986 and on March 1, 1988 it was again renewed for another period of two years commencing March 30, 1988.
