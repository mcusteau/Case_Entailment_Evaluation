Between August 2001 and July 8, 2004, the date of the last elections, the I.T.U.M. Council was represented by Chief Rosario Pinette and councillors Céline Bellefleur, Georges-Ernest Grégoire, Marcelle St-Onge, Gilles Jourdain, Ronald Fontaine, Maurice Vollant, Albert Vollant, Brigitte André and Réjean Ambroise. The latter three councillors formed the opposition.
