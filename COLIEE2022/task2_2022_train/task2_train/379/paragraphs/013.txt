However, it appears to me that there are a number of legal issues, or issues of mixed fact and law, that should be addressed. For example, what is the precise definition of the group to which the Applicants say they belong? Does such a group constitute a social group within the meaning of that term in the Convention refugee definition? For purposes of the Convention refugee definition, must the perpetrators of persecution be a government or government-like organization or can they be individuals or a single family?
