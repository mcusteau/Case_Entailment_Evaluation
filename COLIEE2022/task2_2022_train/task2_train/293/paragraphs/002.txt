The Applicant (Mr. Utovac) was a Statistics Officer at the Income Security Programs Branch of Human Resources Development Canada (HRDC) from January, 1981 until his retirement on December 24, 2002. Effective June 15, 1995, his position was classified in the Social Science Support (SI) group at level SI-03. The work description for the position varied with departmental reorganization, but at all material times included the establishment and escalation of benefit rates for income security programs such as Old Age Security (OAS) and the Canada Pension Plan (CPP), which affected in excess of 4.5 million recipients nation-wide at a value approximating 40 billion dollars annually.
