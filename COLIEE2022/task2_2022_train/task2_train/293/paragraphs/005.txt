Section 91 of the
Public Service Staff Relations Act
, R.S.C. 1985, c. P-35 (repealed by S.C. 2003, c. 22, s. 285) (
PSSRA
), provides an internal grievance scheme for work-related disputes between the employee and the public service employer. Chapter Four of the
Treasury Board Manual
, the "Classification Grievances Policy" (Policy), sets out the grievance procedure for employees who are dissatisfied with the classification of the duties they perform as assigned by the employer. Such grievances must be conducted in accordance with "Appendix B, Classification Grievance Process".
