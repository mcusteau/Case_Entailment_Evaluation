In his affidavit, the visa officer under the factor "language" states that he applied the criteria that are found in factor 8 of Schedule I of the
Immigration Regulations
. He awarded three units for reading, three units for writing and zero unit for speaking English. Yet, the applicant has been living and working in the United States for some six and a half years and dealing with restaurant customers in the English language. Moreover, the interview with the visa officer was carried out entirely in English. That assessment of zero for speaking English is patently unreasonable.
