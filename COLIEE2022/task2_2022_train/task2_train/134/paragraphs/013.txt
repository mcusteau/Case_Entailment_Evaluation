  The PRRA officer also gave little probative value to the letter from the Sri Lankan attorney detailing with the arrest and detention of the applicant's wife. Indeed, she found it to be illegible, of poor quality and containing spelling errors. She noted that there is no indication of how the applicant had it in his possession. Moreover, the PRRA officer could not see why the applicant would be detained as a result of his wife's arrest for possession of a forged passport.
