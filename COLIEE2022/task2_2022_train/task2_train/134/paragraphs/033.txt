  It is difficult to understand why the officer did not address these findings. The least that can be said is that she conducted a very selective reading of this document. No explanation was given as to why the officer disregarded this document in concluding that the applicant has an IFA in Colombo. After all, this is a most credible source, and the leading refugee agency in the world. As so often repeated by this Court, the officer's burden of explanation increases with the relevance of the evidence to the disputed facts:  
 Cepeda-Gutierrez 
   
 v. 
   
 Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration) 
  (1998), 157 F.T.R. 35 at para. 17.
