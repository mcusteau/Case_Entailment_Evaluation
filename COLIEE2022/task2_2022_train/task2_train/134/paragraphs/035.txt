  Finally, the applicant submits that the PRRA officer misinterpreted a DFAIT report which advises Canadian against all non essential travel. While I find it disingenuous to argue that it is only meant to advise Canadians and does not apply to citizens of Sri Lanka, as if the country were not as dangerous for them as it is for Canadians and permanent residents of Canada, I agree with the respondent that this advisory could be interpreted as discouraging travel to the north and east only.
