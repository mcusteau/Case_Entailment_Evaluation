  In its decision, the RPD first reviews various issues which affected the credibility of Mr. Nagamany with respect to the merits of his claim. For various reasons, the RPD did not believe that Mr. Nagamany was arrested and detained in 1988 nor that he lived in Vavuniya from 1995 to 1997. It did not believe either that he identified members of the LTTE.
