  In October 1997, he learned that his girlfriend had again been arrested. He immediately left Sri Lanka for France where he made a refugee claim which was ultimately denied. In April 2000, he attempted to come to Canada on a false passport but was caught by the Austrian authorities and returned to France. He tried a second time but was caught again and returned to France. On his third attempt, he succeeded in coming to Canada and filed his claim upon his arrival on October 1, 2002.
