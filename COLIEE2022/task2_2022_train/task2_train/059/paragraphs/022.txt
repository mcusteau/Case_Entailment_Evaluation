  In addressing this question, the Board referred to the factors cited in the decision of the Federal Court in  
 Sahin v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration) 
 , [1995] 1 F.C. 214; 85 F.T.R. 99 (T.D.). That is, the Board examined the reasons for Mr. Romans' detention, the length of time involved, any delay or lack of diligence on Mr. Romans' part, and possible alternatives to detention.
