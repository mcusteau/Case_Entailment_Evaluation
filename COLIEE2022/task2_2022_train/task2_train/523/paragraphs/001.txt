Rothstein, J.
: This is a motion by the plaintiff to stay the defendant's counterclaim. The plaintiff has commenced an action in this court against the defendant for unpaid freight charges of $21,613.75 U.S. in respect of a shipment of a roll forming machine and parts therefor, used in refrigeration and cooling systems from Gothenburg, Sweden, to Concord, Ontario. The defendant has defended and counterclaimed. The counterclaim asserts that only a portion of the machinery which the plaintiff was transporting was delivered and that the equipment was damaged in transit resulting in a loss to the defendant of some $750,000 Cdn.
