Referring to the applicant's affidavit filed in support the respondent argues that even at the time the applicant submitted his refugee claim he was not totally convinced he wanted to stay in Canada; that the impetus for his decision to "commit himself to staying into Canada" was not his fear of persecution in Mexico but rather the initiation of a serious relationship in Canada; had the applicant really feared for his security and his life as alleged, this concern should have taken precedence over other considerations.
