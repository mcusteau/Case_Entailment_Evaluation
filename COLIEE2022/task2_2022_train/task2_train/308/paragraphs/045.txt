The moving party must place before the court on a review motion a full report from the supervising solicitor of the execution or executions to which it relates, including a description of all goods, equipments and records seized. In this case, the Anton Piller order was executed on January 7 and January 12, 1998 in Halifax, Nova Scotia. Full reports from the supervising solicitors appear in the materials filed. Those reports append complete inventories of the materials obtained during the execution of the order.
