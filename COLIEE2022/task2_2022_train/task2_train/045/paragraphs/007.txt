  The overarching standard of review for H&C decisions is reasonableness ( 
 Mooker v Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration) 
 , 2008 FC 518). The issues of proper legal test applied and procedural fairness are to be assessed under the correctness standard ( 
 Gurshomov v Canada (Minister of Public Safety and Emergency Preparedness) 
 , 2010 FC 1212).
