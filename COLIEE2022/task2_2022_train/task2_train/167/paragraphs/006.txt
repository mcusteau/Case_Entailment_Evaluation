  The expert opinions of the Medical Officers, with respect to the diagnosis and prognosis, are not open to review by this court. However, the Medical Officers' opinions concerning the issue of whether a person's medical condition will create excessive demands on the provision of health and social services in Canada is open to review:  
 Ahir v. Minister of Employment and Immigration 
  (1983), 49 N.R. 185 (F.C.A.);  
 Hiramen v. Minister of Employment and Immigration 
  (1986), 65 N.R. 67 (F.C.A.). The grounds for review include: incoherence or inconsistency, absence of supporting evidence, or failure to consider the factors stipulated in s. 22 of the  
 Regulations 
 :  
 Gao v. Minister of Employment and Immigration) 
  (1993), 61 F.T.R. 65; 18 Imm. L.R.(2d) 306 (T.D.), at 318 [Imm. L.R.].
