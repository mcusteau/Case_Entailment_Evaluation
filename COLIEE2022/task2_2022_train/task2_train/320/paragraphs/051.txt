The Respondent submits that the Adjudicator's decision to reduce her award by 20%, on the basis of contributory fault was not patently unreasonable. The jurisprudence supports the entitlement of an Adjudicator to reduce damages to reflect the degree to which an employee is partially at fault in the events leading up to dismissal; see
Hollett v. Air Atlantic Ltd.
