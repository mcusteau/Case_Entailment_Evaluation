During the interview, the Applicant was also questioned about the receipt of other gifts from Mr. Birchall. She acknowledged receipt of discarded clothing and old jewellery, as well as a glass ornament which she subsequently returned. She also said that she had given Mr. Birchall gifts at Christmas, such as CDs. She did not address the subject of two other cheques, each in the amount of $200, dated July 7, 2001 and October 18, 2002, until Mr. Crowther asked about them. The Applicant said that the first cheque was a birthday gift and the second, for Christmas. The Applicant said that she did not receive all cash gifts from Mr. Birchall.
