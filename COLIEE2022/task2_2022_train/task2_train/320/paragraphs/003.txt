The Applicant is a chartered bank, constituted under the laws of Canada with branches across the country, including a branch in Comox, British Columbia. The Respondent was a Customer Service Representative/Cash Control Officer, known colloquially as a bank teller, at the Comox branch. As the result of a complaint made between February and April 17, 2003, the Respondent was called for an interview with Area Manager, Ms. Jane Black and Mr. Paul E. Crowther, a member of the Corporate Investigation Services on April 17, 2004. The Respondent signed an "Acknowledgment of Rights" form, dated April 17, 2003.
