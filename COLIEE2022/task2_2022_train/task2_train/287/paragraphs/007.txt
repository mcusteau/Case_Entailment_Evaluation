Under rule 408(1) every pleading must contain a precise statement of the material facts on which the party pleading relies. Rule 415(1) provides that every pleading must contain the necessary particulars of any allegation. Further, rule 412(2) states that the raising of a question of law or an express assertion of a conclusion of law shall not be accepted as a substitute for a statement of the material facts on which the conclusion of law is based.
