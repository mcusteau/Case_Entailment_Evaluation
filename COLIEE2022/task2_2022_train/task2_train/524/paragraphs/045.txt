I turn then to consider whether, on the facts of this case, exemplary or punitive damages should be awarded. I note first that there is no statutory impediment to assessing punitive damages in addition to profits or damages calculated in the usual manner. With respect to the Copyrighted Works, the Court's ability to award punitive damages in addition to an election to statutory damages is enshrined in s. 38.1(7) of the
Copyright Act
