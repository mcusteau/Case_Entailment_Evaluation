In
MacKay
, the applicant veteran was injured in a truck accident in 1958. He was briefly hospitalized at the time, but sought no further treatment until 1988. A medical report concluded that the neck pain was reasonably attributable to the 1958 accident. Mr. Justice Teitelbaum held that the Board erred in failing to accept the uncontradicted medical report as new evidence, absent an adverse credibility finding.
