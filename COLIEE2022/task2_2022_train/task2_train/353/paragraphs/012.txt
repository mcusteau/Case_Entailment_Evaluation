Firstly, I do not believe the procedure followed meets the common law requirements of fairness. Substantial interests of applicants for the recording of claims are at stake in such a process. There is no ready means for seeking review of the refusal to record once that decision is taken. Therefore it was incumbent on the Acting Mining Recorder here to give the applicant Aurchem or its representative an opportunity to know what concerns were raised by the inspection report and to respond to those concerns if possible.
