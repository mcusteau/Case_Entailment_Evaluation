In his written argument counsel made two submissions in support of the application for judicial review of the visa officer's decision. The first was that it was unreasonable for the visa officer to conclude that Mr. Madan's work experience did not match the duties of a financial manager. Furthermore, the refusal to issue the visa was procedurally unfair because the visa officer had not invited the applicant to elaborate and clarify the precise nature of his employment duties so as to enable him to make an accurate determination of whether they fell within the NOC's description of the duties of a financial manager.
