Title 1111.2, "Accountants", lists under the sub-heading "Education/Training", "5+, 6+, 7+ R." These numerical indicators refer to: vocational or specific training programs (5); college or technical school diplomas (6); and undergraduate degrees (7). The "+" indicates that additional requirements must be met over and above education and training, which could include extensive experience and demonstrated ability. "R" means that some regulated requirements exist for that group.
