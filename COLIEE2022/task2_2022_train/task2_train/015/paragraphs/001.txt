 Snider, J. 
 : The Applicant, Mr. Fadi Atwani, a citizen of Lebanon, became a permanent resident of Canada on June 14, 2006. On July 28, 2009, he applied for Canadian citizenship. In a decision dated February 24, 2011, a Citizenship Judge denied the application on the basis that she was not satisfied that the Applicant had accumulated the required 1,095 days of residence in the four years (1,460 days) immediately preceding the application date. The Citizenship Judge concluded that the Applicant had not met the requirement for residency under s. 5(1)(c) of the
