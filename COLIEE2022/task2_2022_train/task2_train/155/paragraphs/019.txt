  The respondent made a preliminary motion in this case on January 24, 2013, under section 87 of the  
 IRPA 
 , to prohibit the disclosure of the information that was redacted when the certified tribunal record was created in this application for judicial review. The respondent maintained that the disclosure of this confidential information would be injurious to natural security or endanger the safety of other persons. After reading the redacted passages and hearing the respondent's representations,  
 in camera 
  and  
 ex parte 
 , the Court granted the motion by way of an order issued on February 19, 2013.
