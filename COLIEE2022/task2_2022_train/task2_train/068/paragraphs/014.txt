  I believe that the statutory scheme then under consideration is somewhat different from the one before me. As the Court of Appeal had stated earlier in  
 Calder et al. v. Minister of Employment and Immigration 
 , [1980] 1 F.C. 842; 31 N.R. 56; 107 D.L.R.(3d) 738, at p. 853: 
 
 
 
 "The authority conferred by s. 57 is not confined to the reconsideration of decisions, as such, but is an authority to reconsider 'any claim' in respect of which benefit has been paid or should have been paid."
