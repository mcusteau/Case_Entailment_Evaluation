A trademark which has been in use for a long time is presumed to have made an impression which must be given some weight. (
Pink Panther Beauty Corp.
, supra at 260). However, there is little likelihood of confusion when two trademarks have been used concurrently for a long time without evidence of actual confusion between them. (
Multiplicant Inc. v. Petit Bateau Valton S.A.
