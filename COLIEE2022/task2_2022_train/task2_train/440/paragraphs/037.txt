As of the date on which this application for judicial review was filed, and continuing through to the date of the hearing of this application before me, I am satisfied that the respondent committed reviewable error in her processing of the principal applicant's application for landing. For ease of refer­ence, subsection 46.04(8) is repeated here:
"(8) An immigration officer shall not grant landing either to an applicant under sub­section (1) or to any dependant of the applicant until the applicant is in pos­session of
a valid and subsisting passport
or travel document or a satisfactory ident­ity document." (emphasis added)
"(8) Tant que l'intéressé n'est pas en pos­session
d'un passeport ou d'un document de voyage en cours de validité
ou de pap­iers d'identité acceptables, l'agent d'immi­gration est tenu de lui refuser, ainsi qu'aux personnes à sa charge, le droit d'établisse­ment." (Je souligne)
