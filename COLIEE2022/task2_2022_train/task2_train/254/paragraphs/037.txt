I believe it can now be said that it is settled that it is the date of the application and not of the assessment which must be taken into consideration to determine the number of points to be awarded. In
Wong v. Minister of Employment and Immigration
(1986), 64 N.R. 309, at p. 311, Mahoney, J.A., stated "an application for an immigrant visa is made when it duly initiates the process leading to the issue or refusal of the visa and not only when that processing is committed to the particular official authorized to dispose of the application".
