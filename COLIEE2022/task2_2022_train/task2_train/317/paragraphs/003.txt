In December 2001, the Applicants partnered with Comunicon to bid for a government contract with respect to a data system. Alejandro Segura Walls (Segura) and Anselmo Pardo Lorencez were the co-owners of Comunicon. Comunicon was awarded the contract, but later allegedly conspired with the Government agency to terminate the Applicant's contract. The Applicants, as per a sub-contracting agreement with Comunicon, was expecting to get 80% of the proceeds.
