The Applicant began his military service in the Reserve Force in October 1974 and went on to serve in the Regular Force in April 1976. He retired in March 1995 after nearly 19 years of service in the Regular Forces. During his military career, he served in the infantry and worked as an airframe mechanic. He also played the bagpipes in the military band. The Applicant claims that his current hearing loss and degenerative disc disease of the lumbar spine are attributable to events that occurred during his military service.
