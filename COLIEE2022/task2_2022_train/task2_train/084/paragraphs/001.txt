 Mainville, J. 
 : This case concerns a judicial review submitted by Susheel Malik (the "Applicant"), a citizen of India, concerning a decision of a visa officer dated March 3, 2009 and made in New Delhi, India determining that the Applicant did not meet the requirements of subsection 11(1) of the  
 Immigration and Refugee Protection Act 
  (the "Act") since he did not meet the criteria set out in the  
 Immigration and Refugee Protection Regulations 
  (the "Regulations") pertaining to the federal skilled worker class.
