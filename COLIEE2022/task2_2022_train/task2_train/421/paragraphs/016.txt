The plaintiff's wife, Mrs. Abenstein, has testified that she was present in the truck with the plaintiff and observed the plaintiff's encounters with Sergeant Stanley on October 17, 18 and 19, 1985. Mrs. Abenstein has given evidence consistent with the plaintiff's description of what transpired on those days. She has further testified that on Sunday October 20, 1985, she telephoned Lieutenant Gregory, to whom the plaintiff was to report with respect to this contract, to find out what further was required of the plaintiff before he could return to picking up garbage from the station. Mrs. Abenstein states that she was informed by Lieutenant Gregory that if she and the plaintiff were willing to comply with the hours for pick-up stipulated in the contract, they could return to work.
