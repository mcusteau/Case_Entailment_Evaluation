In this case the defendant relies upon the general conditions set out in DSS 9076 as part of the contract and in the statement of defence relies upon alleged default by the plaintiff as grounds for termination. Particulars of that default are described by the statement of defence as follows:
"(a) The plaintiff did not arrive at Canadian Forces Station Beausejour prior to 4:30 p.m. on October 16, 1985, to collect the garbage and refuse;
"(b) The plaintiff did not collect the garbage and refuse at Canadian Forces Station Beausejour at all on or about October 17, 18, and 19, 1985;
"(c) The plaintiff failed to comply with certain rules specified by the Station Construction Engineering Officer regarding the plaintiff's approach to the Station and communications with staff at the Station."
