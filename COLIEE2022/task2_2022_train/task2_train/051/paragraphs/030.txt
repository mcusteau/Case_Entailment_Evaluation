  Second, without deciding the point, I accept that by virtue of PGL's shareholding in PDGL it may not have been reasonable to expect PGL to be wound up until the determination of the lawsuit or until some corporate reorganization took place. However, by itself, this could not be determinative of the claim to an ABIL as the question to be asked was whether, as of December 31, 1993, it was reasonable to expect that notwithstanding the existence of the lawsuit PGL would be dissolved or wound up and that it would not then begin to carry on business.
