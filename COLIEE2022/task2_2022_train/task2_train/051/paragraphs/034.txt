  Finally, the evidence before the Director was that the CCRA had been informed that PGL continued to make GST credit claims because PGL was the proper company to claim these credits. In my view, claiming GST credits in respect of expenses PGL had paid, as well as continuing to operate a bank account, was equally consistent with the orderly gathering in of assets as part of the winding up process. The Director did not dispute the taxpayer's assertion that PGL had ceased all business activity in 1993.
