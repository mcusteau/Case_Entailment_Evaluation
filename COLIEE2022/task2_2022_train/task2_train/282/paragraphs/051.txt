The Applicant argues that the RPD made a baseless inference in concluding that he did not experience fear of persecution in Pakistan because of when he chose to leave. The Applicant explained that he did not think the situation was sufficiently bad for him to leave until he learned about the false charges that had been laid against him and the police raid on his house. Immediately after these events occurred, he left the country. He left the country three days after he learned of the events that caused him a high level of subjective fear.
