  In  
 Puccini v. Director General, Corporate Administrative Services, Agriculture Canada 
 , [1993] 3 F.C. 557; 65 F.T.R. 127 (T.D.), cited by Justice Muldoon in the passage quoted above, the Court held that s. 18.1(2) and Rule 1602(4) contemplated a specific decision or order in respect of which judicial review was sought. They could, however, also encompass a situation, or involve an ongoing situation, where a number of decisions are taken.
