 : This is an appeal from a decision of the Registrar of Trademarks dated December 15, 1997, which rejected the appellant's Opposition to Canadian Trademark Application No. 722,545 filed by the respondent Monsanto Canada, Inc. on February 11, 1993. The application was to register a trademark consisting of: 
 
 
 
 "1. The trademark is shown in the attached drawing and consists of the colour white applied to the whole of the visible surface of the tablet. The representation of the tablet shown in dotted outline does not form part of the trademark."
