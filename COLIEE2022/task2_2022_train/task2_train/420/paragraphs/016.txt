Fauteux, J., concurred in the result with Taschereau, J., Like Taschereau, J., he focused on the voluntariness of the payment of tax and not the protest. In
Bain v. Montreal (City)
(1883), 8 S.C.R. 252, cited by Fauteux, J., it was held:
"... a protest is of no avail when the payment or execution of the obligation is otherwise voluntary."
