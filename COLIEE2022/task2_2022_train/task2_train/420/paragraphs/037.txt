Although a finding that the s. 68 limitation period is applicable to the plaintiff may appear harsh, it should be remembered that limitation periods are well known in the law and where statutes prescribe procedural steps and time limits, there must be compliance with them. Of course, in some circumstances, legislation confers a discretion on a court or tribunal to extend time. And in exceptional cases such as
Premier Mouton
, there may be other reasons why a limitation period would not be applicable. But that is not the case here.
