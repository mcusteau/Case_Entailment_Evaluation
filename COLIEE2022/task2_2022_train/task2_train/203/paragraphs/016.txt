  Jun Yan relies on the decision of this Court in  
 Gangadeen v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration) 
 , [2000] F.T.R. Uned. 452; [2000] F.C.J. No. 1539 (T.D.), where McKeown, J., held following the decision of McGillis, J., in  
 Savvateev v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration) 
 , [1999] F.C.J. No. 922; 170 F.T.R. 317 (T.D.), that once a specific request to consider section 11(3) is made, the visa officer cannot ignore it, and this even if the reasons given by the applicant to justify the exercise of the discretion are not very clear.
