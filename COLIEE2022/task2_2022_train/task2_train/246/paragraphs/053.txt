The Court's jurisprudence has confirmed that Justice Mactavish's finding in
Kurukkal
that the doctrine of functus officio does not apply to humanitarian and compassionate decisions is also applicable to immigration officers considering applications under the skilled worker category:
Medina
, supra,
Malik v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
, 2009 FC 1283, and
Sharma v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
, 2009 FC 786.
