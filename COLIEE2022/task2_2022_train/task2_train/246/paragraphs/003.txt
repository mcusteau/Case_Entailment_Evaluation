Mr. Marr is a 49-year-old Scot baker. He applied to come to Canada as a skilled worker in June 2007. Mr. Marr completed 11 years of education as well as a two-year post-secondary credential from the Glasgow College of Food Technology, as certified by the City and Guilds of London. He also completed a three-year apprenticeship but failed to include a letter evidencing his apprenticeship with his application.
