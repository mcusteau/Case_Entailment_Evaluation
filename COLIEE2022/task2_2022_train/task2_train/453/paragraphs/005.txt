The reasons for decision are brief. The officer says that in making the decision, the submissions made by the applicants and their counsel were reviewed along with information available in the Field Operating Support System ("FOSS"). The officer concluded that the evidence provided by the applicants failed to establish sufficient grounds to warrant an exemption from the normal requirements of the Immigration and Refugee Protection Act, S.C. 2001, c. 27 ("IRPA").
