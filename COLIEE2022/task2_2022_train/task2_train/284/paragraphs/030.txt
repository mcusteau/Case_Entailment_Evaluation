Accordingly, the court holds that: either s. 6(9) of the Canadian Fisheries Protection Act commands the Crown to make full restitution for the proceeds of the sale, meaning the full monetary value of the sale; or such restitution is subsumed in the monetary sum to be awarded as compensation for the Crown's tort of wrongful conversion.
