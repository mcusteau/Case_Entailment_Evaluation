  The Applicants fear returning to Albania because of a blood feud in the male Applicant's family. His uncle killed a man and the victim's family is seeking to kill a male member of the male Applicant's family. As a result, the male members of the Applicant's family live in hiding. The Applicants fear being returned to the scene of the blood feud and came to Canada to seek refugee status.
