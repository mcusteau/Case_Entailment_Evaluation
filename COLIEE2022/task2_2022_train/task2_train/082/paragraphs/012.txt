  The prescribed factors for the purpose of assessing whether the respondent is a person whose identity has not been established are set out in the IRPR. Section 244 of the IRPR has prescribed separate factors which  
 shall 
  be taken into consideration when assessing whether a person is (a) a flight risk; (b) a danger to the public; or (c)  
 is a foreign national whose identity is not established 
 .
