 McGillis, J. 
 : The applicant was a candidate in a closed competition for the position of head gardener in the Department of National Defence at CFB Esquimault in Victoria, British Columbia. The Selection Board, which consisted of two individuals, assessed the five candidates on their performance in a written examination. One of the members of the Selection Board assessed the candidates for personal suitability, a qualification in the Statement of Qualifications which was to be rated on sub-factors of alertness, reliability and co-operativeness. All of the candidates received identical marks on each of the sub-factors of personal suitability. The applicant ranked second in the competition behind the successful candidate. The eligibility list issued by the Public Service Commission contained only the name of the top candidate from the competition.
