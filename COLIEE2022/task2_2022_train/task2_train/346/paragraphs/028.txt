The courts have held that customs of a band are practices "which are generally acceptable to members of the band upon which there is broad consensus." (See
Bigstone et al. v. Big Eagle et al.
(1993), 52 F.T.R. 109 (T.D.), per Justice Strayer at p. 8; see also
Bone v. Sioux Valley Indian Band No. 290 Council et al.
(1996), 107 F.T.R. 133 (T.D.), per Justice Heald at paragraph 26;
Chingee et al. v. Chingee et al.
(1998), 153 F.T.R. 257 (T.D.), per Justice Reed at paragraphs 12-13.)
