Cullen, J.
: This is an application for an interlocutory injunction restraining the defendant, by its officers, servants, workmen, agents or otherwise, until final disposition of this action, from
(i) selling, distributing, or advertising, or causing to be sold, distributed or advertised, a wine beverage or any alcoholic beverage in association with the trademarks WILDBERRY and WILDBERRY & Design or any trademark confusingly similar thereto; and
(ii) using trademark registration No. 325,412 in a manner likely to have the effect of depreciating the value of the goodwill attaching thereto.
