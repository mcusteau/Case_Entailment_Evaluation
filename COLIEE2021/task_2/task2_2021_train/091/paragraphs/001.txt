 Dawson, J. 
 : Huseyin Tolu is a Turkish citizen who claims a well-founded fear of persecution in Turkey based on his nationality as a Kurd, his Alevi religion, and his political opinion. He brings this application for judicial review from the March 9, 2001 decision of the Convention Refugee Determination Division of the Immigration and Refugee Board ("CRDD") that he is not a Convention refugee.
