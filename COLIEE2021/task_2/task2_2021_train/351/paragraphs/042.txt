Accordingly, Mr. Justice Heald's determination that a voluntary departure amounts to execution of a removal order must be read in light of the provisions as they stood then. Now, the

As the applicant did not seek the Minister's consent, it cannot be said that the SIO erred in holding that the applicant had not executed his removal order by voluntarily leaving Canada.
