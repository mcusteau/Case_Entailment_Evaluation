  In order to bring the information in question within the exemption set out in paragraph 20(1)(b), the applicant must establish, on a balance of probabilities, that the information: 
 
 
 
 1) is financial, commercial, scientific or technical information; 
 
 
 
 2) is confidential information; 
 
 
 
 3) was supplied to a government institution by the third party;  
 and 
 
 
 
 4) was treated consistently in a confidential manner by the third party. (Emphasis added)
