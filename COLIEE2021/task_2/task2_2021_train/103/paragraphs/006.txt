  At the time the Respondent travelled to Canada, she was the holder of a document issued by the German government called an unbefristete Aufenhaltserlaubnis (a permanent residence permit). A permanent residence permit was first issued to the Respondent in 1994 and had been renewed several times. When the Respondent came to Canada, her German residency permit was valid until December 31, 2000.
