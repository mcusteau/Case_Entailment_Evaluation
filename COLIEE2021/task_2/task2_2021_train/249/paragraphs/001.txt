Cullen, J.
: This is an application for judicial review of the decision of the visa officer in Australia, George Sutherland (hereinafter, the "visa officer"), dated July 18, 1996, refusing the applicant's application for permanent status (hereinafter, "AFL") in Canada. The grounds of the refusal are that, because the applicant's son is alleged to fall within the class of inadmissible persons as described in s. 19(1)(a)(ii) of the
Immigration Act
, the applicant's family can be expected to cause excessive demands on social services in Canada.
