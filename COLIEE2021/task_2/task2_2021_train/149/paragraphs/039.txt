  Although the RPD in its decision does refer to events which took place after June 1995, it also refers to the fact that killings and mistreatments of civilians were taking place since before 1986. It also deals with other types of activities of the LTTE such as the massive deportation of Muslims (60,000 to 100,000) which it calls an "ethnic cleansing" in 1990.
