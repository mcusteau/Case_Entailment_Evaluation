  The principal refugee claimant is Ms. Elmancia Dezameau (the "applicant"), a citizen of Haiti. Her children's claims are dependent on hers. Her two younger children, Germa and Alex Malivert, are citizens of the United States whereas her oldest child, Geraldine Malivert, is a citizen of Haiti. Since arriving in Canada, the applicant has had a fourth daughter who is now two years old.
