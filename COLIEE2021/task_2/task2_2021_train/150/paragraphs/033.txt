  It is evident that each concert is separately negotiated and each has its own contract. In some cases MLSE pays royalties on behalf of the promoter, in others it does not. SOCAN takes the position that each contract must be examined in detail focusing on such aspects, among others, as MLSE's control or influence over the performance, its financial interests in the performance, its efforts to pass off responsibilities to the promoters, its indifference to the rights of SOCAN and its members.
