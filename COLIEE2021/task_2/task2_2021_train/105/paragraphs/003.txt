  Attached to the March 8, 2001 letter were several documents the Agency was considering for disclosure in response to the Request. Included in those documents were leases executed on various dates by commercial retail tenants at Jasper Park Lodge and the Applicant ("Retail Leases") and two agreements, dated April 1969 and February 1982, between Her Majesty the Queen in Right of Canada and Canadian National Railway Company ("Crown Leases"). The Crown Leases are in respect of lands in Jasper National Park on which Jasper Park Lodge is located.
