  The standard of review of the Minister's exercise of discretion on questions of fact pursuant to section 4.8 of the  
 Aeronautics Act 
  has been found to be one of reasonableness. This includes the involvement of the Advisory Body charged with making security clearance recommendations. See  
 Clue v Canada (AG) 
 , 2011 FC 323 [ 
 Clue 
 ] at para 14;  
 Russo v Canada (Minister of Transport, Infrastructure and Communities) 
 , 2011 FC 764 at para 20; and  
 Fradette v Canada (AG) 
 , 2010 FC 884 at para 17.
