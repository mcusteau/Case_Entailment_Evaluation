  The tribunal as noted considered exhibits P-11, P-12 (the letters from the Awami League) and P-15 (the letter from his lawyer in Bangladesh) observing none of them made reference to the detention. The tribunal characterized the detention as a "major element of the past persecution and also justifies the fear of further persecution by the police, it is surprising that it is not mentioned in the above documents disclosed by the claimant". As noted, concerning the arrest and detention the tribunal pointed to his answer that he never had any problem with the police.
