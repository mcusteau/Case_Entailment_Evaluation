Thereafter, Hitachi Maxco Ltd. and Rich Palm Enterprise Corporation Ltd. filed a Statement of Claim in the Federal Court against Dolphin Logistics Company Ltd., Blue Anchor Line, Kuehne & Nagel Ltd. and Kuehne & Nagel Inc., alleging that all of them were the "operators, managers, charterers and utilisers" of the YM Prosperity and carriers. The usual allegations that the defendants had failed to safely load, stow, handle, carry, care for, discharge, store and deliver the plaintiffs' cargo were advanced. They also alleged that they were in breach of contract, obligations imposed by law and were concurrently liable in tort.
