  The applicant argues that his right to procedural fairness was breached when the PRRA officer considered a document emanating from the UK Home Office without telling him and without providing him with an opportunity to comment on it. This document, it is to be noted, is not found in the national documentation package of the Immigration and Refugee Board (the IRB). Moreover, it is not a recognized human rights report but a policy document for UK asylum officers which provides clear recommendations on most categories of Sri Lankan claims considered by the UK system.
