The issue before the Adjudicator was whether the CFIA's decision to remove Dr. Frazee from performing condemnation inspections for six weeks was administrative or disciplinary in nature. That was an issue of mixed fact and law which required an examination of both the purpose and effect of the employer's action. It required the Adjudicator to apply the largely undisputed evidence of what took place to a set of accepted standards or legal principles which define discipline in the employment context.
