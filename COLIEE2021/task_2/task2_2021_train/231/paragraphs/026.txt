Finally there are the March 9, 1993 decisions denying the grievances of the applicants. These decisions state in part:
"... comments from your solicitor have been considered in the response to this grievance. CSC considers the information received regarding the escape plot to be reliable. Disclosure of the identity of informants will not be provided. I support the decision of transfer you to the Special Handling Unit.
