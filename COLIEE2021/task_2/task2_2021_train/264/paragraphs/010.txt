Subsection 74(2) of the
Act
provides that the IAD, in granting a stay of a deportation order, may impose terms and conditions. Subsection 74(3) of the
Act
provides that the IAD may, at any time, amend the terms and conditions, impose new ones or cancel the stay. Subsection 33(3) of the
Rules
provides that where the IAD reviews a case of its own motion pursuant to subsection 74(2) the registrar shall notify the parties of the review 30 days before the date fixed for the review.
