However, even if unreasonable delay by the Minister might, in an appropriate case, give rise to a serious issue for stay purposes (which I do not need to decide here), when an applicant has been arrested for purposes of deportation and deportation is imminent, the fact that the Minister has not considered a subsequent humanitarian and compassionate application, is not a reason to hold up deportation.
