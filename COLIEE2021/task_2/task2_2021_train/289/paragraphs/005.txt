Finally, the applicant has been in hiding, evading an immigration arrest warrant for two and one-half years. She is now in custody to the detriment of her own liberty interest and at a cost to the respondent. By virtue of her being in custody, she and her children (for whom no detention order was issued but who appear to be with her voluntarily) are separated from her husband. Having regard to these considerations, the balance of convenience favours the Minister.
