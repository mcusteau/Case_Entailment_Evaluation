  In this case, the applicant theoretically had the right to appeal to the IAD, but in practice it was a meaningless right insofar as she wanted the IAD to grant an exemption based on humanitarian and compassionate considerations under section 25 of the IRPA. The IAD did not have jurisdiction on this issue, and thus the applicant's appeal would have been dismissed since it is not disputed that Viasna cannot be sponsored in the family class (given that he was not declared).
