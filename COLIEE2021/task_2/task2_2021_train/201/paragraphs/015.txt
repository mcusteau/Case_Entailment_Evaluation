  The Court must be able to control the proceedings that are before it so as to prevent abuse. In this regard, a party's lack of status should normally have been decided prior to the hearing on the merits by means of a motion to strike, if necessary. Although there was no judicial determination on the merits, the first judge authorized the application for judicial review and the second, who was to hear the case on the merits, granted the applicant leave to commence this proceeding out of time, i.e. almost 11 months after the application for leave and judicial review dated December 8, 2009, was filed with the Court.
