  The applicant's submission in this respect is essentially that, in her reasons, the officer did not adequately address all of the risk factors that he had alleged he would face if returned to Gaza. However, his argument in support of that submission is based largely on the evidence that was not before the officer, evidence which, I have concluded, must be struck from the record in these proceedings. Accordingly, I have given it no weight in reviewing the officer's decision.
