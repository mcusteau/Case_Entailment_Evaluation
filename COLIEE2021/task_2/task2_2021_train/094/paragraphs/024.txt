  The applicant has requested that the Court certify as a serious question of general importance the question certified in Varga, above: "[w] hat obligation, if any, does a PRRA Officer have to consider the interests of a Canadian-born child when assessing the risks involved in removing at least one of the parents of that child?" An appeal is pending on that question. The respondent is opposed to the certification of this or any question as, in its view, this case turns on its facts.
