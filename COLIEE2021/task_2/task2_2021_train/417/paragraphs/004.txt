The respondent Band is a First Nations Band duly constituted under the
Indian Act
, R.S.C. 1985, c. I-5 (the Act). The government structure, procedures, and custom elections of the respondent Band are governed by the
Customary Election Regulations of the Sucker Creek First Nation #150A
(the Election Regulations). The Election Regulations provide for the election of one Chief and six Councillors to act as representatives of the respondent Band for a term of three years.
