As the business of Cantex prospered and grew, it paid off its obligations to Northland and the loans made by William. But Cantex was expanding beyond the rental business and becoming involved in road building and consulting engineering. Cantex's need for gravel led to the acquisition of the "Wiltse lands", yet another occasion for a point of contention between the brothers. Each asserts that he "found" the land, but nothing substantive turns on that.
