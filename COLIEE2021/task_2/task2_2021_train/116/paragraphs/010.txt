  On June 22, 2010 the Advisory Body unanimously recommended to the Director that Mr. Clue's TSC be permanently revoked on the following basis: 
 
 
 
 The Advisory Body was unanimous in its recommendation to cancel the transportation security clearance. A further review of the entire contents of the file, including police reports about his involvement in criminal activities at the airport and the applicant's affidavit led the members of this Advisory Body to believe that on a balance of probabilities this individual may be prone or induced to commit an act that may unlawfully interfere with civil aviation. The applicant's affidavit and previous submissions did not provide sufficient information that would compel this Advisory Body to recommend re-instating his clearance.
