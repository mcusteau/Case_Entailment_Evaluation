  The applicant submits that it is open to the PRRA Officer to make her own assessment of state protection. However, the PRRA Officer erred by ignoring voluminous documentary evidence that is highly corroborative of the applicant's claim that he will face persecution without hope of state protection should he be forced to return to Hungary. The sole reference in the reasons to the plight of the Roma in Hungary was the PRRA Officer's statement that, "It is acknowledged that there are some concerns with respect to living conditions for Roma in Hungary."
