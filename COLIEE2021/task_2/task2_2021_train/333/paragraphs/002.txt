The appellant is the owner of Registration No. 129,933 for the trademark MacGregor registered in association with "frozen fish and fillets thereof, smoked fish and fillets thereof and smoked kippered herring". In response to a request submitted by the respondent, the Registrar of Trademarks, on November 28, 1985 issued a notice pursuant to section 44 of the Trade Marks Act, addressed to the appellant. On April 11, 1986, the appellant filed the affidavit of I.R. Whitman, Vice-President-Sales of the appellant, along with Exhibit A, in response to the said section 44 notice. Following receipt of that affidavit, both parties made submissions in writing to the Registrar.
