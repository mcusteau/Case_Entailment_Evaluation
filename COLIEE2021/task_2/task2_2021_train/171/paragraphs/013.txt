  I appreciate that the Court is entitled to consider the Investigator's Report as constituting the reasoning of the Commission. As stated by the Federal Court of Appeal in  
 Sketchly 
 , supra, at paragraph 37: 
 
 
 
 "The investigator's report is prepared for the Commission, and hence for the purposes of the investigation the investigator is considered to be an extension of the Commission. When the Commission adopts an investigator's recommendations and provides only brief reasons, the Courts have rightly treated the investigators report as constituting the Commission's reasoning for the purpose of the screening decision ..."
