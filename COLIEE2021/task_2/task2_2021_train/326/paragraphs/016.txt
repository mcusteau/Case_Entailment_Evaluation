On June 28, 2004, Konrad Sioui accepted the terms of an agreement contained in a letter that chief Rosario Pinette had sent him on June 23, and which reiterated the terms of resolution 64. On July 5, 2004, Mr. Sioui thus received a payment of $58,557.63. The agreement also included the payment of a monthly housing allowance of $600 until the end of his contract.
