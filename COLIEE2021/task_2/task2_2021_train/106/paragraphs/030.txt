  Decisions regarding motions to strike pleadings from a statement of claim start from the premise that material facts pleaded must be taken as true, unless the allegations are based on assumption and speculation: Operation Dismantle Inc. v. Canada, [1985] 1 S.C.R. 441, (1985) 18 D.L.R. (4th) 481 at para. 27.
