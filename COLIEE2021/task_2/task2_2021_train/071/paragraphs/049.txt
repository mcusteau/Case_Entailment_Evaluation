  As for the five-year ban on obtaining new passports, in my view, the adjudicator's determination on this point is also reasonable. The imposition of a penalty is a highly discretionary element of the decision, and its length is certainly within the range of possible, acceptable outcomes (and coincides with the length of penalties in other cases that have been upheld by this Court such as in  
 Okhionkpanmwonyi v Canada (Attorney General) 
 , 2011 FC 1129 at paras 8-9, 207 ACWS (3d) 316).
