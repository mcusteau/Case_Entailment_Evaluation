  The applicants assert that the adjudicator's decision was made without regard to the evidence. Throughout their Memorandum of Fact and Law, they posit alternative theories and explanations for what they suggest is more likely to have happened to their passports. They also offer explanations for the inconsistencies in their statements that led the investigators to conclude that their credibility had been undermined. However, as noted, none of these explanations was given to Passport Canada or to the adjudicator.
