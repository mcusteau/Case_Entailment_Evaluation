 , [2001] F.T.R. Uned. 361; 2001 FCT 583;  
 Aguebor v. Ministre de l'Emploi et de l'Immigration 
  (1993), 160 N.R. 315 (F.C.A.);  
 Kanakulya v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration) 
 , [2002] F.T.R. Uned. 679; 2002 FCT 1063;  
 Muhammed v. Minister of Employment and Immigration 
  (1993), 67 F.T.R. 152 (T.D.);  
 Suresh v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration 
 , [2002] 1 S.C.R. 3; 281 N.R. 1; 208 D.L.R.(4th) 1. I can only overturn the decision if it is entirely unsupported by the evidence or made without regard to the evidence.
