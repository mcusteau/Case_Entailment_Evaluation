The Minister submits that the Appeal Division specifically acknowledged that the natural parents could continue to have a relationship with the child after the adoption, and that it stated quite clearly that an adopted child could continue to love her natural parents without that becoming a barrier to finding that a genuine relationship of parent-and-child exists between adoptive parent and the child. Obviously, the Board did not require that the natural parents sever their ties with the child. Rather, it was the existence of authority in one relationship, and the absence of it in the other which the Board considered to be relevant.
