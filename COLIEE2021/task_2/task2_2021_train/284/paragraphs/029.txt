Thus, to the extent that the Crown adopts the position that s. 6(9) of the Coastal Fisheries Protection Act exacts return to the plaintiffs of a sum less than the full monetary value of the seized fish, to that extent the Crown becomes liable for the tort of wrongful conversion, as Parliament ordained in s. 3(1)(a) of the Crown Liability Act.
