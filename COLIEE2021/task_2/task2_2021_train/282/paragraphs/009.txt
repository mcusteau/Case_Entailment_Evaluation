In 2004, the Applicant retired and returned to his place of birth. He became more politically active and acquired official membership of the UKPNP in August 2004. In November 2004, he became the Vice President of UKPNP for the Bosagala unit. He was in charge of disseminating the party's literature, and he made sure that the party's slogan and banners were hung in public places. As a result of his work, more people joined the party.
