On April 23, 2006, the Bosagala unit of the UKPNP met to discuss Seyab Khalid's threat and decided it was serious. In response, they decided to publicize the threat to the people in the area at a procession on April 25, 2006. They met on April 24, 2006 to discuss the arrangements for the procession. After the meeting, the Applicant did not go home because he expected retaliation from the government. That night, the police raided his house, destroyed his belongings, insulted his family and demanded to know his whereabouts. His family immediately informed him of the attack, so he left his hometown and stayed in the village of Mong for the night. The next day, he left for Rawalpindi.
