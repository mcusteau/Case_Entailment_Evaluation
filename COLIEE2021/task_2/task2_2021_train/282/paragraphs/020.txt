From Rawalpindi, the Applicant contacted Sardar Mohammad Altaf Khan, the president of the UKPNP unit in Thorar, for an update on the situation. He was told that the police were looking for him and wanted him "on any cost." He contacted his lawyer for advice and was told that a case was registered against him for working against the solidarity of the country and for giving a bad name to the Pakistan army and its agents. He was told that his life was in danger and he would be arrested if he came back.
