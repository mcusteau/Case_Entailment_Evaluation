The applicant is originally from Sri Lanka. He fled there to come to Canada in August 1986, making a claim for refugee status at that time. He heard nothing further with respect to his claim until 1992 when he was asked to fill in a Personal Information Form and informed his claim would proceed in accordance with the backlog provisions. The applicant was advised that he had been determined to have a credible basis and that he was able to apply for permanent residence in Canada. He made a formal application for permanent residence on June 23, 1992.
