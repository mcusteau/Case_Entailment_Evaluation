  The applicant was hired by the Mint as a senior accountant in the Head Office Accounting Section on September 30, 1985. The applicant claims that, from the start of her employment at the Mint, she was sexually harassed by a senior employee of the Mint. The applicant also claims that she had been sexually harassed by another senior employee since mid-1987. The applicant exhausted internal avenues of redress at the Mint but was not satisfied. In the fall of 1989, she contacted the Commission and spoke to Michelle Bougie, an investigator. The applicant complained to the Commission by letter on November 6, 1989.
