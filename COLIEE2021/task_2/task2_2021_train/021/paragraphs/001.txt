 Joyal, J. 
 : This is an application for judicial review, pursuant to  
 s. 18.1 of the  
 Federal Court Act 
 , of a decision made by the Canadian Human Rights Commission (hereinafter, "the Commission") in which it was decided to close the applicant's file with respect to her sexual discrimination complaint against the Royal Canadian Mint (hereinafter, "the Mint"). The applicant seeks an order setting aside this decision, an order directing the Commission to conduct a full and complete investigation of the complaint,  
 as amended January 23, 1990, with such directions as this court considers to be appropriate, and such further and other relief as this Honourable court deems just.
