  The applicant submits that, with respect to her complaint against the Mint, the Commission did not properly exercise its authority to investigate complaints pursuant to s. 43 of the  
 Canadian Human Rights Act 
 . It failed to interview some witnesses. It took cognizance of Mr. Justice Chadwick's judgment. It failed to consider the investigator's recommendation that the issue go to conciliation. In addition, the applicant submits that the Commission refused to give reasons for its decision to close the applicant's file.
