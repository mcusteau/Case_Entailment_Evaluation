  The applicant applied to be transferred to Canadian custody to serve the remainder of both his outstanding Canadian sentence and his American sentence. Prior to being transferred to Canadian custody, CSC advised the applicant that if he returned to Canada he would serve a life sentence in Canadian custody with a commencement date of April 18, 1985. On July 17, 2003, the applicant signed a Verification of Consent to Transfer to Canada to serve the remainder of his sentence.
