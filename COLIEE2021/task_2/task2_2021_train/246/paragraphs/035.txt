It is clear in this case that Mr. Marr has the educational credential referred to in s. 78(2)(d)(i) but he does not have the years of study. What points is he to be awarded for education? Subsection 78(4) instructs that he "shall be awarded the same number of points as the number of years of completed full-time or full-time equivalent studies set out in the paragraph or subparagraph." The meaning of this seemingly simple phrase has perplexed officers, judges and applicants alike.
