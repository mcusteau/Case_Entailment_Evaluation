Respondent submits that the authority for varying the points awarded for occupational demand from time to time flows from the
Immigration Act
itself (R.S.C. 1985, c. I-2). Section 7 requires the Minister after consultation with the provinces concerning regional demographic needs and labour market considerations, and such organizations as he deems appropriate, to lay before Parliament annually a report specifying the number of immigrants the Government deems appropriate to admit to Canada during "any specified period of time", and the manner in which demographic considerations have been taken into account.
