The plaintiff, Marine Atlantic Inc., is a Crown corporation which operates passenger, auto and freight ferry services throughout Atlantic Canada and to the State of Maine under contract to the federal government. It receives funding from Transport Canada and reports directly to the Minister of Transport. The defendant 381383 Ontario Limited is an Ontario corporation which carries on business as "Blyth & Company". The defendant Blyth & Company Travel Limited is also a company incorporated pursuant to the laws of Ontario which operates under the name of "Blyth & Company Travel". At all material times, the defendant Sam Blyth was acting as an officer and representative of the defendant companies.
