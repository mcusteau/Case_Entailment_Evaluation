  Shortly thereafter, Ms. Velez claims that she was contacted at work and told that someone from the FARC would come to get her in a week because the commander was anxious to see the first list of the co-operative's investors. As a result, she moved to Caicedonia with her aunt and made plans to leave the country. She fled Colombia on March 22, 1999 and shortly thereafter entered the United States on a visitor's visa, which expired in the fall of that year.
