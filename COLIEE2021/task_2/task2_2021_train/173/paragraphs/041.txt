  Accordingly, in my view, it was entirely appropriate and correct for the RPD to assess these issues and to rely on its findings in respect of them in rejecting the Applicants' claims. In finding that the documentary evidence did not support the Applicants' claims, the RPD was simply stating that it was not reasonable to believe that there was a serious possibility that the Applicants would still be persecuted or harmed by members of the FARC if they were to return to Columbia at this time.
