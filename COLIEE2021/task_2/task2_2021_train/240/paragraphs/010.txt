It is well settled that it is not the role of the Federal Court, on judicial review, to substitute its decision for that of the Refugee Protection Division even if it might not have reached the same conclusion: Singh v. Canada (Minister of Employment and Immigration) (1983), 3 D.L.R. (4th) 452 (F.C.A.); Oparanyamele v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration), 2005 FC 1247 at para. 40 (F.C.T.D.).
