  In bald terms, the petitioner submits that it is not the legitimate purpose of s. 10(1)(a) of the Parole Act that is here in question, but rather the threshold determination as to whether such statute, as written, falls within the field of exception of s. 1. He submits that a statutory provision which is as arbitrary or unreasonable as s. 10(1)(a) of the Parole Act, as applicable to this case, falls outside the exception.
