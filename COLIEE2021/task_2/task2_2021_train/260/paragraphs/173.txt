The inventors had to devise new processes to produce levofloxacin because they were unable to obtain it from the racemate using the standard techniques of the day. The common general knowledge was, therefore, of no assistance in obtaining levofloxacin when applied to the teachings of the Ofloxacin references. (Hayakawa Affidavit at paras. 24-25, AR, v. 4, Tab 6, pp. 713-714.)
