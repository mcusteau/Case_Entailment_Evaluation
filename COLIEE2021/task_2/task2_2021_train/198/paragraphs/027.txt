  The translator, Mr. Yang, admitted that he used a list of questions to facilitate the process of recording the refugee claims. Although he said he only used the list for a short time, the Board held that it appeared as though the list was used from February 2002 until mid-May 2004 (Decision at page 5). The list of questions is found at page 299 of the Certified Tribunal Record. The order of the questions is noteworthy, and accords with the skeletal nature of the PIFs that were produced.
