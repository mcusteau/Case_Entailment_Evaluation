The Board reviewed the evidence adduced by the parties in its reasons. With respect to Mr. Glowczewski's affidavit, the Board noted that despite the "abundance of documentary evidence" there was very little information about the periods during which the packaging samples attached as exhibits were used. The Board stated that it had to rely on the cross-examination of Mr. Glowczewski in order to attempt to establish the dates when some of the packaging or advertising flyers were used.
