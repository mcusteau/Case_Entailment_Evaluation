  The police came to the applicant's house looking for her husband, searched the house without authority, and when they found he was not there interrogated the applicant. The applicant did not tell the authorities that her husband had left the country. The stress of the interrogation caused the applicant to have a miscarriage. After she was released from the hospital she was summoned to the police office under false pretences and questioned about her husband's activities and whether she intended to leave the country.
