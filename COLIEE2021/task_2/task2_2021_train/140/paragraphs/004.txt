  The couple is originally from Mexico. The principal applicant worked as a hairdresser and travelled most of the time to do his work. He had clients from every sector of society. In the course of his work, he discovered that men connected to the authorities (the Treasurer for the Federal District of Mexico; a police commander) were involved in illegal activities (drug trafficking). In short, the applicant knew and saw things that he should not have known or seen. This had terrible consequences for him. He was kidnapped, threatened, persecuted.
