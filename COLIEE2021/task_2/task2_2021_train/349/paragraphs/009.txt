On March 28, 1994 an application for judicial review was filed by the applicant, seeking to quash the decision of the visa officer and seeking an assessment as an "electronic engineer" as of the date of the application, January 22, 1993. By letter dated April 20, 1994, the respondent agreed to a consent Order quashing the visa officer's decision of May 18, 1993 and requiring that a different visa officer consider the applicant's application for permanent residence.
