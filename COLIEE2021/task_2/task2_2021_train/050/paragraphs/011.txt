  The respondent submits that just because the applicant is a medical doctor does not imply that he will necessarily meet the employment requirements set out in the NOC for different occupations in the medical field. It is submitted that it was reasonable for the visa officer to award the applicant zero points for experience because the officer was not satisfied that the applicant met the employment requirements for clinical perfusionists or that he had performed a substantial number of the main duties as specified by NOC 3214.
