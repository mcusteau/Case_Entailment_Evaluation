As a final general statement, it is useful to underline the limitations of review under s. 18 of the Federal Court Act. This is not an appellate review. To succeed the applicant must do more than establish the possibility that I might have reached a different conclusion than the visa officer in this assessment. There must be either an error of law apparent on the face of the record, or a breach of the duty of fairness appropriate to this essentially administrative assessment.
