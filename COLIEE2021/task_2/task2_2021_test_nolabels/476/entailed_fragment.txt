In matters of assessment of the evidence by the panel, the applicable standard of review is reasonableness (
Dunsmuir v. New Brunswick
, 2008 SCC 9, [2008] 1 S.C.R. 190;
Uppal v. Canada (Minister of Citizenship and Immigration)
, [2009] F.C.J. No. 557 (F.C.) (QL)). The panel's findings with regard to a refugee claimant's credibility call for deference from the Court
