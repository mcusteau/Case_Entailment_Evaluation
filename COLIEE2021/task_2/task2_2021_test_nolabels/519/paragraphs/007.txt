Lastly, the respondent's open-ended offer to cause a review of the applicant's situation for the purpose of assessing whether humanitarian and compassionate grounds exist, is a relevant factor. An order for an extension of time is a discretionary remedy. The applicant has had an alternative route offered to him, by the respondent, which if it had been pursued would have resulted in effectively giving him the remedy he seeks. Indeed this route is still open to the applicant, if he proceeds expeditiously.
