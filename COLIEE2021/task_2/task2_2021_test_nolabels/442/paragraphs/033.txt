In the present case, the Applicant filed a complaint with the Commission, alleging that she had been treated in a discriminatory manner by the Respondent in the context of employment, resulting from the failure of the Respondent to employ her junior driver, Mr. Love, because he was driving a truck owned by a woman, that is, the Applicant. According to the material provided by the Applicant to the Commission, the alleged discriminatory act occurred in June 1997, but she did not contact the Commission until October 1998.
