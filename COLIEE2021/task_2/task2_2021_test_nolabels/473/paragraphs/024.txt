The principal issues on the application can be summarized as follows:
1. Whether the Board erred in relying on the audiograms conducted during the Applicant's military service as evidence that the Applicant's hearing loss was not attributable to his military service; and
2. Whether the Board erred in finding that the Applicant's degenerative disc disease was not caused by an injury sustained during military service.
