In the present case, the Board does not accept the applicant's claim that he was ever involved in an accident, let alone injured, while on duty because nothing exists in the applicant's service records indicating he was in an accident and injured. In its reasons, the Board comes close to saying that a veteran is not entitled to a disability pension in the absence of an injury report in his service records.
