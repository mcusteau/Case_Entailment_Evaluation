In April 1992, trial of the action was scheduled, on the joint application of counsel, to commence October 26, 1992, and it was expected to last some 15 days. In September 1992, counsel for the plaintiff filed a notice of constitutional question, indicating that the plaintiff intended to question the constitutional validity of an administrative order of the Canadian Forces, and also of s. 39 of the
Canada Evidence Act
under which it was said the defendant had withheld disclosure of material relevant to this action on the basis of Cabinet privilege. That notice was addressed to the Attorney General of Ontario, the Attorney General of Canada and to counsel for the defendant, Her Majesty the Queen.
